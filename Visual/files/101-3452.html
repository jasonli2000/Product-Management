NAME PSB BCBU PATIENT MOVEMENT
ITEM TEXT PSB TRACK PATIENT MOVEMENT
DESCRIPTION 
This Protocol is used to track the patient admissions, discharges and 
other Movements for BCBU Contingency. Every time a patient movement 
activity happens a message is sent to the BCBU Contingency Workstation to 
keep it current with the patient location.
