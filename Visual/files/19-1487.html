MENU TEXT Close Out PM Work Orders
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT CLOSE OUT PM WORK ORDERS
DESCRIPTION 
Close out a PM work list entry by entry. User is asked for a complete
specification of the first PM work order; after that the system assumes
the shop, month, and type (MONTHLY or WEEKLY) of work list.
