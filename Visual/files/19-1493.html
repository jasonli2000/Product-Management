ENEQINV1 (1493)    OPTION (19)

Name Value
NAME ENEQINV1
MENU TEXT CMR Inventory (EIL)
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT CMR INVENTORY (EIL)
DESCRIPTION 
This option generates the Equipment Inventory Listing (EIL) report.
Equipment that is belongs to the user-specified CMR and is accountable
will be printed on this report. A page suitable for the signature of the
Responsible Official is printed when the output is directed to a printer.
 
Accountable equipment is equipment with an Investment Category of either
CAPITALIZED/ACCOUNTABLE or NOT CAPITALIZED/ACCOUNTABLE.
 
See the Non-EIL Inventory report to print equipment that belongs to a CMR
but does not meet the other criteria for inclusion on the Equipment
Inventory Listing report.
CREATOR USER,ONE
