MENU TEXT Biomedical Engineering Resource Survey
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT BIOMEDICAL ENGINEERING RESOURC
DESCRIPTION 
Driver option for Biomedical Engineering Resources Survey module. This
module collects data on how individual facilities maintain their
biomedical equipment and instrumentation. Data from all sites is
aggregated once a year by the Engineering Service Center in St. Louis.
