DG TRANSFER PATIENT (1890)    OPTION (19)

Name Value
NAME DG TRANSFER PATIENT
MENU TEXT Transfer a Patient
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT TRANSFER A PATIENT
DESCRIPTION 
This option is used to transfer a patient between wards, to/return from
Authorized/Unauthorized/Authorized Absence less than 96 hours and to/
return from Absent Sick in Hospital (ASIH).  It is also used to transfer
a patient from Authorized Absence to Unauthorized Absence.  In the case
of an Interward or ASIH transfer the user will also be prompted with the
"TREATING SPECIALTY TRANSFER DATE" prompt where s/he may transfer the
users treating specialty and/or provider if either changed at the time
of transfer.
