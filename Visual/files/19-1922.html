UPPERCASE MENU TEXT SUPERVISOR ADT MENU

DESCRIPTION 
This option is used to define ADT parameters, wards, ward statistical data
(G&L Initialization), device output parameters, DRG trim points, and
treating specialties.  It is also used to view G&L corrections, recalculate
the cumulative G&L totals and generate a listing of treating specialties
within your specific medical center.
