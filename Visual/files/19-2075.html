NAME DG INSURANCE LIST
MENU TEXT Insurance List of UNKNOWNs for Inpatients
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT INSURANCE LIST OF UNKNOWNS FOR
DESCRIPTION 
Lists the inpatients who have "Unknown" in their insurance file or have
left that question unanswered. The major sort is by division with a 
choice of a current listing, those patients who are currently inpatients
or those patients admitted for a specified date range.
SSN, DOB, VET status, service connection and admission date/time are all
displayed. Totals are also displayed for each division, showing the number
of "Unknowns" and the number who have not answered that question.
A final total page shows the breakdown of each division and the final
Medical Center totals.
