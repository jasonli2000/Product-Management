MENU TEXT Hold Printing a Follow-up Letter
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT HOLD PRINTING A FOLLOW-UP LETT
DESCRIPTION 
This option holds the printing of a follow up letter until the letter
is released.
 
NOTE: This option does not hold the printing of bills or charges on
      the patient statement.
