MENU TEXT Verification
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT VERIFICATION
DESCRIPTION 
 The 'Verification' option is used by Pharmacists to verify prescriptions
which are in the 'Non-Verified' status; obtain a listing of those remaining
'Non-Verified' scripts; or calculate the number of 'Non-Verified' scripts
by either Patient or entering Clerk.
