UPPERCASE MENU TEXT DRUG COST REPORT (132 COLUMNS)
DESCRIPTION 
This report lists dispensing information for IV drugs. The report may 
be  run for a single drug, all drugs, non-formulary drugs, a specific 
category of drugs, a VA class of drugs, a specific IV type, or by cost.
The report may be run for a single or all IV Rooms, and may also include 
ward and patient dispensing data.
