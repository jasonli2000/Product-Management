DESCRIPTION 
  This report captures the total IV dispensing cost for a drug, by ward.  
Your are asked to enter the start date (date to start capturing data), and
a stop date (date to stop capturing data). At the "Select DRUG" prompt, 
you may enter a specific drug, "^ALL" for all drugs, "^NON" for all
non-formulary drugs, "^CAT" for a category of drugs, or "^VADC" for a
specific drug class.  At the "Select WARD" prompt, you may enter the
name of a specific ward, "^ALL" for all wards, or "^OUTPATIENT" for
outpatient locations only.
