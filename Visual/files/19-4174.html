Name Value
NAME IB TP FLAG OPT PARAMS
MENU TEXT Flag Stop Codes/Clinics for Third Party
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT FLAG STOP CODES/CLINICS FOR TH
DESCRIPTION 
This option is used to flag stop codes and clinics as either non-billable
for Third Party billing or to be ignored by the Third Party auto biller.
These parameters are all flagged by date and may be inactivated
and re-activated.
