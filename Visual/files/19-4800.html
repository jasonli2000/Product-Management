NAME GMRYMGR
MENU TEXT Patient Intake/Output Menu
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT PATIENT INTAKE/OUTPUT MENU
DESCRIPTION 
This is the main menu for the Intake and Output(I&O) application.  It
contains options for: (1) tracking patient intake and output, (2) starting
and discontinuing IV lines and fluids, (3) documenting IV care, and (4)
printing various intake and output reports.
