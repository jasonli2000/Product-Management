OUT OF ORDER MESSAGE GMRV*5*19 - shutdown Vitals CHUI
DESCRIPTION 
This option allows data to be entered for temperature, pulse, respiration
and detailed blood pressure.  Detailed blood pressure captures the numeric
value of both the systolic and diastolic pressures and also associates
(a) the site where the pressure was taken (e.g., left arm vs right arm),
and (b) the position of the patient when the reading was taken (e.g., 
sitting vs standing).
