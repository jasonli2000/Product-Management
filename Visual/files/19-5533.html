NAME ECTF CONTRACT
MENU TEXT Contract Summary by Fiscal Year
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT CONTRACT SUMMARY BY FISCAL YEA
DESCRIPTION 
The contract summary report prints for a user selected fiscal year.
The data is sorted by responsible service and within responsible
service, by contract number.  Fields displayed include: contract
number, description, contract type (i.e. maintenance, service,
or sharing), and contract amount.  A sub-total by service is
computed, as well as a grand total.
