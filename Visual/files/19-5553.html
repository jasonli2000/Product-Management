NAME ECTMGR
MENU TEXT DHCP Interim Management Support
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT DHCP INTERIM MANAGEMENT SUPPOR
DESCRIPTION 
This option is the primary menu driver for the DHCP Interim Management
Support software.  The purpose of the package is to provide 
management at the VAMC with easily accessible, reliable, and
useful information about the center, its workload, and its
resources.  The data is primarily from the DHCP software
applications and associated files.
