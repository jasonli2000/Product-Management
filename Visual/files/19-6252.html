ONCO FOLL-ADD CONTACT (6252)    OPTION (19)

Name Value
NAME ONCO FOLL-ADD CONTACT
MENU TEXT Add Patient Contact
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT ADD PATIENT CONTACT
DESCRIPTION 
Upon selection of a patient, the program called pulls over the data
from MAS that is relevant to patient contacts: names and addresses of the
patient, next of kin (NOK), next of kin-2 (KIN), and the guardian. The
information is 'pulled over' and defined in the contact file, and
displayed, permitting the user to edit the data present in the contact file,
or to add new contacts not already defined.  The user has the choice of
continuing with this patient and making an attempt at follow-up, or to
continue adding contacts for another patient.
