ONCO STAT-SURVIVAL TX (6263)    OPTION (19)

Name Value
NAME ONCO STAT-SURVIVAL TX
MENU TEXT Survival by Treatment
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT SURVIVAL BY TREATMENT
DESCRIPTION 
Providing there is sufficient data, life tables and survival curves
can be produced using Treatment or Sequence of Treatment as a
variable. Treatment groups are defined, and search templates may
be used to further refine the group studied.
