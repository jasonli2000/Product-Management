NAME ESP VIOLATIONS BY OFFICER
X ACTION PRESENT YES
MENU TEXT Violations Issued By Officer
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT VIOLATIONS ISSUED BY OFFICER
DESCRIPTION 
This submenu option allows VA Police staff to print out a list of
U.S.D.C. Violation Notices and Courtesy Warning Notices issued.
The Violations Issued By Officer option allows VA Police staff to
sort by a specifc time period, a specific officer, and several other
sorts alternative.
