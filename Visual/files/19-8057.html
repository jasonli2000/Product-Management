NAME SCRPW ENCOUNTER ACTIVITY RPT
MENU TEXT Encounter Activity Report
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT ENCOUNTER ACTIVITY REPORT
DESCRIPTION 
Option for producing encounter, visit, and unique patient statistics by
selected clinic, provider, or stop code perspective.  In summary format
output can be ranked alphabetically or by frequency of encounters, visits,
or uniques.  Detailed format allows the selection of specific clinics,
providers, or stop codes and will produce frequency ranked statistics of
diagnosises and procedures recorded in addition to encounters, visits and
uniques.
