LRMINEWD (819)    OPTION (19)

Name Value
NAME LRMINEWD
MENU TEXT Verification of data by tech
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT VERIFICATION OF DATA BY TECH
DESCRIPTION 
Allows lab techs to check and approve their own result entries or
supervisors to approve lab techs' result entries (LRSUPER key is
required to verify data entry by others).  The lab tech or supervisor
must choose a field to edit, then those accessions that have been edited
will be displayed.  The lab tech or supervisor can exclude specific
accessions from review, they can review the data as the wards or lab will 
see it, and they can exclude accessions from verification.  The approved 
results can also be sent to a device near the requesting location.
