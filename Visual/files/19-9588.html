SPNL CONT OF CARE OUTCOMES (9588)    OPTION (19)

Name Value
NAME SPNL CONT OF CARE OUTCOMES
X ACTION PRESENT YES
MENU TEXT Continuum of Care Outcomes
UPPERCASE MENU TEXT CONTINUUM OF CARE OUTCOMES
DESCRIPTION 
This option controls viewing, editing, and creation of Outcomes records as
part of a patient's continuum of care.  A continuum of care outcome is not
related to a discrete episode of inpatient or outpatient rehabilitation or
an annual evaluation.  In this care type, therefore, the rehabilitation
episode of care model is not utilized.
