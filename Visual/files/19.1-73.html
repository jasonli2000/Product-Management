DG DETAIL (73)    SECURITY KEY (19.1)

Name Value
NAME DG DETAIL
DESCRIPTION 
This key is held by those users who are authorized to enter a date of
death by means other than discharging an inpatient to death or death
with autopsy, i.e., a death which occurs outside the facility.  The
members of this group will also receive mailman notification whenever
a patient expires and who made the death entry.  For those users for
whom you wish only a bulletin generated but do not wish to allow entry
of deaths assign this key but do not allow access to the 'Death Entry'
option.
