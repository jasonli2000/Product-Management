Name Value
NAME 828600 Ambulatory Care Administration
BOC
  1. BOC:   2553 Miscellaneous Contractual Svc for Indigent Veterans
DESCRIPTION 
This includes administrative personnel and costs in the ambulatory 
care and processing section.  It excludes all costs associated with 
burial arrangements, comfort items and patient fund activities, 
which will be charged to cost center 411.  It also excludes direct medical care 
employees and support staff which should be in cost center 204.
