841900 Quality Assurance and Case Mix Activity (841900)    COST CENTER (420.1)

Name Value
NAME 841900 Quality Assurance and Case Mix Activity
DESCRIPTION 
Includes only station level personnel and costs associated with 
Quality Assurance, Utilization Review, Risk Management, MEDIPRO, 
Case Mix and Health Systems Review (HSRO) type activities.  Does 
not include personnel and costs whose activity is confined to 
only one service, such as Medical Service Quality Assurance 
Position.
