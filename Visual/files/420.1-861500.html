861500 Administrative Programs (861500)    COST CENTER (420.1)

Name Value
NAME 861500 Administrative Programs
DESCRIPTION 
All administrative programs, either approved by central office, or regional
office, not related to direct patient care at host facility.  It includes
direct charges for personnel services, consultant services, travel,
contractual services, printing and reproduction, and supplies for administ-
rative medical care programs not related to direct patient care at host 
facility and not identified by other cost centers.  This account is 
restricted to central office approval.
