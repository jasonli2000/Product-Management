NAME 2102 Round Trip Tvl Between Old and New Sta To Seek Perm Res Quarters
DESCRIPTION 
INCLUDES ALL TRAVEL AND TRANSPORTATION EXPENSES OF THE EMPLOYEE AND SPOUSE, OR OF EITHER OF THEM, FOR ONE ROUND TRIP BETWEEN THE OLD AND NEW DUTY STATIONS FOR THE PURPOSE OF SEEKING RESIDENCE QUARTERS. (CHARGEABLE TO LIMITATION.007)
