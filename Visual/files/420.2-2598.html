2598 Contract Hospital and Outpatient Treatment (2598)    BUDGET OBJECT CODE (420.2)

Name Value
NAME 2598 Contract Hospital and Outpatient Treatment
DESCRIPTION 
Includes cost for PHC visits and all amounts paid to hospitals other than contract per diem rate;costs other than for hospital days of care for medical services under 38 U.S.C. 613: all incidental costs paid to others such as surgeons, nurses, etc.
