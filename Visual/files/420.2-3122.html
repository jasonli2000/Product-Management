NAME 3122 Office Automation/Word Processing, Purchased.
DESCRIPTION 
Comprises electromechanical devices which employ or produce punched cards
or paper tapes.  Included devices used in conventional tabulating operation
that support an electronic data processing system; such as car punches,
tabulator,electronic calculators(i.e.,604,607),transceivers, IBM series 50,etc
