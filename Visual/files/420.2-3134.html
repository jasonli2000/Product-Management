3134 ADP Software, Purchased (3134)    BUDGET OBJECT CODE (420.2)

Name Value
NAME 3134 ADP Software, Purchased
DESCRIPTION 
Includes all accessories except the computer itself and ADP Software (as
defined in subaccount 2324) purchased from its owner, with a purchase
price less than $25,000. For purchases greater than $25,000, see
subaccount 3124. Except as provided by the purchase contract, the title of
purchased software remains with the vendor. The Department, its employees
or others included in its behalf may not use, copy, modify, or transfer
the program, or any copy, modification or merged portion, in whole or in
part, except as provided by law or expressly provided for in the software
license. Excluded is software designed to Government specifications or
changes, enhancements or applications made to accommodate specific
Goverment requirements (see subaccount 2515).
