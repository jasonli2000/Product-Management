NAME 4250 Reimbursement for Losses
DESCRIPTION 
Includes amounts restored or otherwise adjusted in accordance with Public
Law 334, 84th Congress;reimbursement to employees for the cost of repairing
or replacing their personal property damaged or destroyed by patients or
domiciliary members while such employees are engaged in the performance of
their official duties in accordance with Public Law 87-574;amounts applicabl
to claims under Public Law 88-558 for damage to or loss of personal
property incident to service;awards arising from abrogation of contracts
