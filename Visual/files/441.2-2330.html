2330 (2330)    FEDERAL SUPPLY CLASSIFICATION (441.2)

Name Value
NUMBER 2330
ABBREVIATED DESCRIPTION TRAILERS
DESCRIPTION 
TRAILERS  **NOTE**  THIS CLASS INCLUDES ONLY COMPLETE TRAILERS, AND
CHASSIS THEREFORE,  ANY END ITEMS, ASSEMBLIES, PARTS, ATTACHMENTS, OR
ACCESSORIES, OTHER THAN COMPLETE CHASSIS, FOR USE IN OR ON TRAILERS
ARE CLASSIFIED IN CLASSES OTHER THAN THIS CLASS.  THE COMBINED CHASSIS
AND BODY OF A SPECIAL PURPOSE TRAILER, SUCH AS A MACHINE SHOP, MOBILE 
LAUNDRY, OR DENTAL LABORATORY, IS CLASSIFIED IN THIS CLASS.  THE 
COMPLETE MOBILE UNIT, CONSISTING OF CHASSIS, BODY, AND ADDITIONAL
EQUIPMENT, AS IN AN EQUIPPED TRAILERMOUNTED MACHINE SHOP, IS EXCLUDED 
FROM THIS CLASS.
INCLUDES:  SEMITRAILERS; SEMITRAILER DOLLIES; CARGO TRAILERS; PASSENGER
AND HOUSE TRAILERS; SPECIAL PURPOSE TRAILERS, SUCH AS AMMUNITION.
EXCLUDES:  FIRE FIGHTING TRAILERS; SPECIALLY DESIGNED TRAILERS FOR USE
IN AND AROUND AIRFIELDS, HANGARS, AND WAREHOUSES.
