DESCRIPTION 
INDUSTRIAL ASSEMBLY MACHINES
**NOTE*8 THIS CLASS INCLUDES EQUIPMENT SPECIFICALLY DESIGNED TO ASSEMBLE
PREFABRICATED COMPONENTS WITHOUT FURTHER DESIGN MACHINING OF THE
COMPONENTS.  EQUIPMENT DESIGNED TO PERFORM COMBINED MACHINING AND
ASSEMBLING OPERATIONS WHICH RESULT IN MATERIAL REMOVAL ARE CLASSIFIED
IN CLASSES OTHER THAN THIS CLASS.  ALSO EXCLUDED ARE ITEMS FOR WHICH
MORE SPECIFIC CLASSIFICATIONS ARE SUITABLE.
INCLUDES:  INDUSTRIAL PRODUCTION TYPE POWER SCREWDRIVING MACHINES; CIRCUIT C
CARD ASSEMBLY MACHINES.
EXCLUDES:  SPECIALIZED AMMUNITION AND ORDNANCE ASSEMBLY MACHINES; WRAPPING
AND PACKAGING MACHINERY; METAL WORKING MACHINERY; HAND TOOLS.
