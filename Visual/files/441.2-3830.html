ABBREVIATED DESCRIPTION TRUCK & TRACTOR ATTACH
DESCRIPTION 
TRUCK AND TRACTOR ATTACHMENTS
INCLUDES:  EQUIPMENT FOR MOUNTING ON TRUCKS AND TRACTORS, SUCH AS
BULDOZERS, AUGERS, BLADES, SNOWPLOWS, SWEEPERS, ROAD MAGNETS, WATER
DISTRIBUTORS, CRANES; WINCHES FOR INTEGRAL MOUNTING ON TRUCKS OR
TRACTORS.
EXCLUDES:  POWER TAKE OFF UNITS; ATTACHMENTS FOR TANKS, SELF-PROPELLED 
WEAPONS, AND HIGH SPEED TRACTORS; A-FRAMES AND WINCHES SPECIFICALLY
DESIGNED FOR TRUCK MOUNTING; CRANES AND CRANE BOOMS FOR WRECKER TRUCKS;
SPECIAL INDIVIDUAL WORK AND POWER SECTIONS OF CONSTRUCTION EQUIPMENT
WITH A QUICK CONNECT/DISCONNECT CAPABILITY.
