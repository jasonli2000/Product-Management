NUMBER 4130
ABBREVIATED DESCRIPTION REFRIG. AND A/C COMPONENTS
DESCRIPTION 
REFRIGERATION AND AIR CONDITIONING COMPONENTS.
INCLUDES:  HEAT EXCHANGER EQUIPMENT, REFRIGERANT STRAINERS, REFRIGERATION
COMPRESSORS, OPERATING COMPONENTS OF REFRIGERATION AND AIR CONDITIONING
EQUIPMENT, PLANTS OR SYSTEMS CLASSIFIED IN 4110 OR 4120.
EXCLUDES:  REFRIGERATION AND AIR CONDITIONING PLANTS (SEE 4110 OR 4120);
COLLECTIONS OF REFRIGERATION COMPONENTS "PACKAGED" AS A UNIT INCLUDING
A REFRIGERATION COMPRESSOR, EVAPORATOR, ETC, SPECIFICALLY DESIGNED AND
TAILORED FOR APPLICATIONS SUCH AS INDIVIDUAL PREFABRICATED WALK-IN TYPE
REFRIGERATORS.  THESE INDIVIDUAL COMPONENTS WILL BE CLASSIFIED IN 4130.
THE "COMPLETE" PACKAGED UNIT WILL BE CLASSIFIED IN 4110.
