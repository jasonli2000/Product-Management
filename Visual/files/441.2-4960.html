NUMBER 4960
ABBREVIATED DESCRIPTION SPACE VEHICLE MAIN REPAIR
DESCRIPTION 
SPACE VEHICLE MAINTENANCE, REPAIR, AND CHECKOUT SPECIALIZED EQUIPMENT
INCLUDES:  CHECKOUT AND TEST EQUIPMENT SPECIALLY DESIGNED FOR USE WITH
SPACE VEHICLES, INCLUDING REMOTE CONTROL SYSTEMS.
EXCLUDES:  CHECKOUT AND TEST EQUIPMENT USED WITH BOTH GUIDED MISSILES
AND SPACE VEHICLES; SPECIALLY DESIGNED INTERNAL (BUILT-IN) CHECKOUT
EQUIPMENT FOR REMOTE CONTROL SYSTEMS; BASIC TYPES OF ELECTRICAL AND 
ELECTRONIC TEST INSTRUMENTS, INCLUDING THOSE SPECIALLY DESIGNED, SUCH AS
AMMETERS, OHMMETERS, MULTIMETERS, AND SIMILAR INSTRUMENTS, AS SHOWN
IN THE INDEXES TO THE FSC.
