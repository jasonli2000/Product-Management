DESCRIPTION 
NOTE: THIS CLASS INCLUDES ONLY PORTABLE EXPANDABLE AND NONEXPANDABLE
UTILIZED SHELTERS THAT ARE DESIGNED TO BE TRANSPORTED BY LAND, SEA, OR AIR.
THESE SHELTERS ATE COMPLETE UNITS THAT REQUIRE NO SPECIALIZED SET-UP
EQUIPMENT AND MINIMUM SITE PREPARATION.
EXCLUDES, FABRIC WALL SHELTERS, AIR SUPPORTED STRUCTURES, REFRIGERATED
BUILDINGS, CARGO CONTAINERS, AND PREFABRICATED SEMI-PERMANENT BUILDINGS
AND STRUCTURES.
