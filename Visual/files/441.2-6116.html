Name Value
NUMBER 6116
ABBREVIATED DESCRIPTION FUEL CELL POWER UNITS
DESCRIPTION 
FUEL CELL POWER UNITS, COMPONENTS AND ACCESSORIES
NOTE: THIS CLASS INCLUDES ITEMS THAT ARE DESIGNED TO PRODUCE AN ELECTROMOTIVE
ELECTROMOTIVE FORCE DIRECTLY FROM AN OXIDATION-REDUCTION TYPE OF CHEMICAL REACTION. A COMPLETE POWER UNIT MUST INCLUDE TANKS, PUMPS, AND ASSOCIATED CONTROL
 EQUIPMENT, AND REQUIRES A CONTINUOUS, UNINTERRUPTED SUPPLY OF LIQUID OR 
GASEOUS FUEL AND OXIDIZERS.
INCLUDES COMPLETE FUNCTIONING FUEL CELL POWER UNITS; FUEL CELLS; FUEL CELL
POWER UNITS; FUEL CELLS; FUEL CELL ASSEMBLIES OR MODULES; FUEL CELL REACTANT
CHAMBERS; FUEL FEED MECHANISMS; ELECTROLYTE HEATERS; HEAT EXCHANGERS; AND
OTHER INTERNAL COMPONENTS PECULIAR TO AND USED EXCLUSIVELY ON OR WITH, FUEL
CELL POWER UNITS CLASSIFIED IN THIS CLASS.
EXCLUDES NONRECHARGEABLE BATTERIES (CLASS 6135); RECHARGEABLE BATTERIES 
(CLASS 6140) GENERATORS AND GENERATING SETS (CLASS 6115) AND ITEMS FOR WHICH
MORE SUITABLE CLASSES ARE SPECIFICALLY PRESCRIBED BY THE INDEXES AND
STRUCTURE OF THE FSC.
