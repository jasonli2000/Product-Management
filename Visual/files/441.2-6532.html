ABBREVIATED DESCRIPTION HOSPITAL & SURGICAL CLOTHES
DESCRIPTION 
HOSPITAL AND SURGICAL CLOTHING AND RELATED SPECIAL PURPOSE ITEMS INCLUDES
ONLY THOSE CLOTHING ITEMS WHICH ARE REQUIRED EXCLUSIVELY FOR USE IN 
HOSPITAL OPERATIONS, OR IN MEDICAL OR DENTAL FACILITIES, WHICH HAVE NO 
FUNCTIONAL COUNTERPART ELSEWHERE IN FEDERAL CATALOG SYSTEM.
EXCLUDES GENERAL PURPOSE ITEMS OF CLOTHING AND FABRICATED TEXTILE MATERIAL.
