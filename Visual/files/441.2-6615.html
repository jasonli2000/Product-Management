DESCRIPTION 
AUTOMATIC PILOT MECHANISMS AND AIRBORNE GYRO COMPONENTS 
NOTE: INCLUDED IN THIS CLASS ARE GYRO COMPONENTS OF GUIDED MISSLES. EXCLUDED
ARE COMPLETE GYRO MECHANISMS AND NONAIRBORNE GYRO COMPONENTS, BOTH OF WHICH
ARE CLASSIFIED IN THE SAME CLASSES AS THEIR NEXT HIGHER ASSEMBLIES.
INCLUDES AUTOMATIC PILOT REGULATORS; DIRECTIONAL, VERTICAL, BANK AND TURN,
AND HYDRAULIC SURFACE GYRO CONTROLS; AIRBORNE AND SHIPBORNE AUTOMATIC 
HPILOT MECHANISMS; HELICOPTER AUTOMATIC STABILIZATION EQUIPMENT. 
EXCLUDES AUTOMATIC PILOT TRAINING DEVICES; AUTOMATIC PILOT TRAINING DEVICES;
AUTOMATIC PILOT MECHANISMS, GUIDED MISSILE.
