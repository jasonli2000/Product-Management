7010 (7010)    FEDERAL SUPPLY CLASSIFICATION (441.2)

Name Value
NUMBER 7010
ABBREVIATED DESCRIPTION ADPE SYS.
DESCRIPTION 
NOTE:  A GROUP OF GENERAL PURPOSE ANALOG, DIGITAL, OR HYBRID
ELECTRONIC OR ELECTROMECHANICAL DEVICES THAT ARE INTERCONNECTED
TO OPERATE AS A SYSTEM.  FREQUENTLY REFERRED TO AS A ADP SYSTEM, 
ALTHOUGH THE TERM "SYSTEM" IS NOT CONSIDERED DEFINITIVE.
INCLUDES AN ASSEMBLY OF DEVICES CONSISTING OF A CENTRAL PROCESSING
UNIT (CPU) AND THE NECESSARY INPUT/OUTPUT DEVICES, ACCESSORIAL
DEVICES, ANALOG MEASUREMENT DEVICES, SOFTWARE AND/OR FIRMWARE
REQUIRED TO PERFORM THE DESIRED OBJECTIVES.  ALSO INCLUDES 
SUBASSEMBLIES AND UNITS IN WHICH INPUT/OUTPUT DEVICES, CPU'S
AND/OR ACCESSORIAL DEVICES OR COMPONENTS ARE INCORPORATED INTO A 
SINGLE ASSEMBLY OR UNIT.  A SPECIALLY DESIGNED DEVICE INCORPORATED
INTO A SYSTEM SHALL NOT INFLUENCE THE CLASSIFICATION OF THE BASE
ASSEMBLY OR UNIT.
GROUP 70
