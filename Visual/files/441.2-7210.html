ABBREVIATED DESCRIPTION HOUSEHOLD FURNISHINGS
DESCRIPTION 
INCLUDES HOUSEHOLD FURNISHINGS OF TEXTILES, PLASTICS, PAPER,
RUBBER, AND OTHER MATERIALS; SHEETS, PILLOWS, BLANKETS, AND 
OTHER BEDDING; INSECT BARS; TABLECLOTHS; TOWELS; STEAMER RUGS;
MATTRESSES; BEDSPRINGS, BOX STYLE (FABRIC COVERED); BEDSPRING AND 
MATTRESS SETS.
EXCLUDES FLOOR COVERINGS; CURTAINS; DRAPERIES; PAPER NAPKINS;
PAPER DOLLIES; BEDSPRINGS, OPEN STYLE (ALL-METALLIC).
