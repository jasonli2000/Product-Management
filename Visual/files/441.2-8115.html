INCLUDES SHOE BOXES; BEER CASES; PILL BOXES; PIANO CASES;
ENGINE BOXES; BOMBSIGHT BOXES.

PRIMARILY FOR SHIPPING, STORAGE, AND HANDLING OF AMMUNITION,
NUCLEAR ORDINANCE, EXPLOSIVES, GUIDED MISSILES, MILITARY
CHEMICAL AGENTS; AND REPAIRABLE/REUSABLE CONTAINERS 
SPECIALLY DESIGNED FOR SPECIALIZED EQUIPMENT SUCH AS THE
"SHIPPING AND STORAGE CONTAINERS" FOR COMPONENTS OF
AIRCRAFT, SPACE VEHICLES, AUTOMOTIVE VEHICLES, SHIPS,
GROUND COMMUNICATION EQUIPMENT.
