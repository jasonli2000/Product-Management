3 (3)    ADMINISTRATIVE CERTIFICATIONS (442.7)

Name Value
NUMBER 3
TYPE FAR 52.214-22 EVALUATION OF BIDS
TEXT 
IN ADDITION TO OTHER FACTORS, BIDS WILL BE EVALUATED ON THE BASIS OF ADVANTAGES
AND DISADVANTAGES TO THE GOVERMENT THAT MIGHT RESULT FROM MAKING MORE THAN
ONE AWARD (MULTIPLE AWRDS). IT IS ASSUMED, FOR THE PURPOSE OF EVALUATING BIDS,
THAT $250 WOULD BE THE ADMINISTRATIVE COST TO THE GOVERMENT FOR ISSUING AND
ADMINISTERING EACH CONTRACT AWARDED UNDER THIS SOLICITATION, AND INDIVIDUAL
AWARDS WILL BE FOR THE ITEMS OR COMBINATION OF ITEMS THAT RESULT IN THE LOWEST
AGGREGATE COST TO THE GOVERMENT, INCLUDING THE ASSUMED ADMINISTRATIVE COSTS.
