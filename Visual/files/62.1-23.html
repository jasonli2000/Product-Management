DESCRIPTION 
Could be used on the test, URIC ACID, under the Site/Specimen of URINE.
After accepting the value for the URIC ACID, the urine volume will be  
prompted.  Upon receiving a positive numeric result the URIC ACID result
will be converted to units/24 hrs.
The calculation is (URIC ACID * VOL/24 HRS) / 100
The result is displayed to 2 decimal places and has a 'c' after it.
