CRITERIA FOR USE 
In Surgery, Trauma or GI bleeding:
   Must have evidence of acute loss of 15% of blood volume
   (1000 ml of blood lost), OR
 
 After Recovery from Surgery, Trauma or GI bleeding:
   Must have a Hemoglobin <8 gm/dl or HCT<24 %, OR
 
 Chronic Anemia:
   Must have a specific diagnosis AND symptoms related to anemia
   (e.g. severe tiredness, fainting).  The following diagnoses are
   usually contraindications to transfusion:
      Iron deficiency, pernicious anemia, nutritional deficiency
      malabsorption syndrome.
