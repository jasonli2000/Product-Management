BRIEF DESCRIPTION ALARM BURGLAR
NOMENCLATURE 
ALARM, DURGLAR (EXCEPT INSTALLED TYPE). A DEVICE DESIGNED TO GIVE AN
AUDIBLE OR VISIBLE WARNING OR RADIATE ABEAM OF INAUDIBLE HIGH FREQUENCY
SOUND, TO WARN OF A THEFT IN PROGRESS OR FORCEABLE ENTRY. IT MAY BE
AC-DC,DC OR BATTERY OPERATED, AND MAY BE AUTOMATICALLY RECHARGEABLE.
INCLUDES, PORTABLE, AND RACK OR WALL MOUNTING TYPE, BUT NOT THE
INSTALLED TYPES. INCLUDES RADIO STYLE THAT TRANSMITS A PRERECORDED
VOICE MESSAGETO POLICE VEHICLES, ETC., OF A CRIME IN PROG RESS.
