BRIEF DESCRIPTION TESTER SMOKE DETECTOR

TESTER, SMOKE DETECTOR. AN ITEM DESIGNED TO TEST SMOKE DETECTORS FOR
SENSITIVITY TO SMOKE OR HEAT AND/OR PROPER OPERATING VOLTAGE BY EITHER
HOLDING AND TESTING THE HEADS OF DETECTORS BY HAVING SMOKE INDUCED INTO
A SMOKE CHAMBER WITHIN THE UNIT, METER WILL PROVIDE AN ALERTING SIGNAL
UPON DEPARTURE FROM NORMAL OPERATING CONDITIONS; OR AT PLACE OF
INSTALLATION SO DETECTOR'S SENSITIVITY IS MEASURED UNDER ACTUAL
ENVIRONMENTAL CONDITIONS IN TERMS OF A DISCRETE ELECTRICAL 
 VOLTAGE READING AND MAY BE READJUSTED FOR ABNORMAL CONDITIONS.
