CYSTOMETER AND IRRIGATOR, UROLOGICAL. AN AUTOMATICALLY OPERATED ITEM
DESIGNED TO MEASURE THE PRESSURE AND ALSO IRRIGATE AND DRAIN THE
BLADDER. CONSISTS OF RESERVOIR MOUNTED ON AN ADJUSTABLE STANDARD AND
BASE, ALONG WITH IRRIGATOR PANEL AND DRAINAGE RECEPTACLE. IRRIGATION IS
PERFORMED BY GRAVITY FROM RESERVOIR AT THE TOP OF THE STAND, REGULATED
BY STOPCOCK, FLUID DRIPS INTO OPEN TUBE, THEN INTO THE BLADDER, WHEN
BLADDERIS FULL OR INTRAVESICAL PRESSURE REACHED DRA INAGE GOES INTO
OPERATION.
