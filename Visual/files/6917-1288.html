DESIGNED TO BE USED IN CONJUNCTION WITH THE HEMODIALYSIS APPARATUS AND

IMPURITIES, CHEMICALS, AND LIQUID ARE DIALYZED FROM THE BLOOD THROUGH

OF TOP AND BOTTOM FRAME, CLAMP UNITS, GASKETS, BLOO D PORT CONNECTORS,
DIALYSATE CONNECTORS AND DIALYZER BOARDS. MAY BE COMPLETE WITH CART OR
STAND.
