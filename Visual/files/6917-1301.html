BRIEF DESCRIPTION ELEC EVKRESP MODULAR
NOMENCLATURE 
ELECTROPHYSIOLOGY SYSTEM, EVOKED RESPONSE, MODULAR. A MODULAR SYSTEM
DESIGNED TO PROVIDE THE MEANS TO STUDY THE RELATION OF THE BODY TO
ELECTRICAL REACTIONS USING A COMBINATION OF EVOKED RESPONSES, SUCH AS
STIMULATION OF THE AUDITORY (EAR), ELECTROMYOGRAPHY (EMG), VISUAL
(EYE), AND SOMATOSENSORY (SKIN) SENSES. GENERALLY INCLUDES SIGNAL
AVERAGER WITH COMPUTATIONAL CAPABILITIES OF EITHER CENTRAL PROCESSING
UNIT OR COMPUTER, DIGITAL; SIGNAL CONDITIONING AMPLI FIERS OR
PRE-AMPLIFIERS; DISPLAY TERMINAL, NONPROGRAMABLE, COMPUTER SYSTEM
(OSCILLOSCOPE); AUDIO AND VISUAL STIMULATORS AND THE LIKE.
