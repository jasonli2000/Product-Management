BRIEF DESCRIPTION INHALATOR SINGLE
NOMENCLATURE 
INHALATOR, SINGLE. BASICALLY IS A BREATHING ASSISTOR. IT IS AN
APPARATUS FOR VENTILATING A PATIENT BY PROVIDING SUITABLE MIXTURE OF
OXYGEN, CARBON DIOXIDE, AND/OR AIR. ITEM IS NOT DESIGNED TO BE USED ON
A PATIENT WHO HAS STOPPED BREATHING. IT CONSISTS OF VARIOUS COMPONENTS,
MAINLY FACE MASK OR MOUTHPIECE, TUBING, GAGES, REGULATOR, CONTROLS, AND
MAY HAVE NEBULIZER AND HUMIDIFIER.
