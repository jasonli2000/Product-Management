BRIEF DESCRIPTION RESUS & INH MOBILE
NOMENCLATURE 
RESUSCITATOR AND INHALATOR, MOBILE. A MOBILE ITEM DESIGNED FOR
INITIATING AND RESTORING RESPIRATION OF A NON-BREATHING PATIENT AND FOR
ASSISTING PATIENT'S BREATHING ONCE BREATHING HAS BEEN RESTORED. IT MAY
BE DESIGNED TO DELIVER FILTERED ROOM AIR, PURE OXYGEN OR A SUITABLE
MIXTURE OF OXYGEN AND AIR. IT CONSISTS OF VARIOUS COMPONENTS, MAINLY
FACEMASK OR MOUTHPIECE, TUBING, GAGES, REGULATOR, CONTROLS, AND MAY
HAVE NEBULIZER AND HUMIDIFIER.
