BRIEF DESCRIPTION INHALER UN GAS ANESTH

INHALER UNIT, GAS ANESTHESIA APPARATUS. AN ITEM DESIGNED FOR USE IN
ADMINISTRATION OF AN ANESTHETIC FOR ALL TYPES OF ANESTHESIA REQUIRING
INHALATIONAL GENERAL ANESTHESIA. IT IS CAPABLE OF ACCURATELY ADDING A
KNOWN PERCENTAGE OF VAPOR TO THE FRESH GASES ON A CONTINUOUS FLOW
ANESTHETIC APPARATUS. THE INCOMING GAS STREAM IS SPLIT INTO A DILUTING
FLOW AND A VAPORIZING FLOW. THE ANESTHETIC IS PICKED UP BY THE
VAPORIZING FLOW ANDAFTER ACCURATE METERING REJOINS THE DILUTING 
 FLOW. MAY BE ACCOUNTED FOR SEPARATELY OR AS A MAJOR COMPONENT OF
ANESTHESIA APPARATUS, GAS.
