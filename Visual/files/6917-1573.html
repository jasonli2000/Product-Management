OF A CONTROL BOX AND RECTAL TAMPON, DESIGNED TO PROVIDE SIMPLE AND
CONVENIENT CONTROL OVER THE PROBLEMS OF INCONTINENCE BY MEANS OF THE

PATIENT'S MUSCLES TO FUNCTION IN A MORE NORMAL CAPACITY.
