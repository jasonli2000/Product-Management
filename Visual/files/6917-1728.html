ALIGNMENT INSTRUMENT, PATIENT-POSITIONING LASER, TELETHERAPY APPARATUS.
AN OPTICAL INSTRUMENT TO PERCEIVE IFTHE BEAM FROM A LASER IS DIRECTED
PROPERLY SO THAT A PATIENT MAY BE ACCURATELY POSITIONED FOR RADIATION
THERAPY. IT PROVIDES AN ACCURATE MEANS OF CORRECTING TRANSLATIONAL AND
ANGULAR MISALIGNMENT ON PATIENT- ALIGNING LASERS AND LIGHTS. ALSO MAY
BE APPLIED FOR TESTS OF ALIGNMENT AND ADJUSTMENT OF OTHER TELETHERAPY
AND SIMULATOR MACHINES.
