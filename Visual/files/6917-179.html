BRIEF DESCRIPTION UNIFRM ISSUE CTRL SYS
NOMENCLATURE 
UNIFORM ISSUE CONTROL SYSTEM. A MICROCOMPUTER CONTROLLED SYSTEM WHICH
ALLOWS AUTHORIZED INDIVIDUALS, WHO AREISSUED A CODED CONTROL CARD, TO
INSERT THE CARD INTO A SPECIFIED SLOT WHICH ACTIVATES THE SYSTEM. AT
THIS TIME, THE INDIVIDUAL MAY RECEIVE A CLEAN UNIFORM OR MAY DEPOSIT
THEIR SOILED UNIFORM. THE SYSTEM MAY HAVE THE CAPABILITY OF MAINTAINING
A RECORD INDICATING THE NUMBER OF UNIFORMS ISSUED AND RETURNED
ACCORDING TO EACH INDIVIDUAL USER'S CARD NUMBER. IT MAY ALSO HAVE THE
CAPABILITY OF INDICATING THIS STORED INFORMATION IN PRINTED FORM.
