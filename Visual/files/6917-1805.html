BRIEF DESCRIPTION TOMOGRAPH IMAG SYSTEM
NOMENCLATURE 
TOMOGRAPHIC IMAGING SYSTEM, COMPUTERIZED. A SYSTEM WHICH UTILIZES
X-RAYS FOR IMAGING LIVING TISSUE. ITS DESIGN ALLOWS FOR SHOWING IN
DETAIL, IMAGES OF STRUCTURES LYING IN A PREDETERMINED PLANE OF TISSUE
WHILE BLURRING OR ELIMINATING DETAIL IN IMAGES OF STRUCTURES IN OTHER
PLANES. IMAGE SIGNALS ARE FED TO ITS COMPUTER FOR PROCESSING AND
DISPLAY ON A CATHODE RAY TUBE (CRT).
