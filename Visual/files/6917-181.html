THAT WASHES, SANITIZED, AND DRIES FIBERGLASS AND METAL LAUNDRY TRUCKS.

CLOSE, WASHING BEGINS, WATER GERMICIDALS AND DETERGENTS SPRAYS AGAINST
THE CART, INSIDE AND OUT, AT PROGRAMMED INTERVALS. AT THE END OF WASH
CYCLE THE AIR DRYER COMES IN. A CLEAN CART EMERGES, SO THEY CAN
TRANSPORT CLEAN LINENS AND LAUNDRY TO SUPPLY 
 PATIENT AREAS.
