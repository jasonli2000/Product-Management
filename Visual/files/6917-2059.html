THAWING-DRYING UNIT, INTRAVENOUS SOLUTION CONTAINERS. A SELF-CONTAINED
ELECTRIC FAN AND RACK UNIT DESIGNED FOR USE BY THE PHARMACIST SERVICE,
IN SUPPORTING AND THAWING FROZEN INTRAVENOUS SOLUTION BAGS OR BOTTLES
(I.V.P.B.S). IN THE THAWING PROCESS, THE FAN GENTLY CIRCULATES ROOM
TEMPERATURE AIR OVER THE FROZEN ADMIXTURES THAT ARE SUSPENDED FROM
HOOKS ON THE RACK. THE FAN IS ALSO USEFUL IN THE DRYING UP OF
CONDENSATION CAUSED BY THE THAWING.
