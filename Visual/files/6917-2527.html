BRIEF DESCRIPTION MONITOR WATER HARDNS
NOMENCLATURE 
MONITOR, WATER HARDNESS. AN APPARATUS WHICH MONITORS, AND AT VARIOUS
TIME INTERVALS WILL PERFORM AUTOMATIC TEST, TO DETERMINE THE DEGREE OF
HARDNESS OF WATER IN AN ION-EXCHANGE WATER SOFTENING SYSTEM (E.G.
BOILERS, PROCESS WATER SUPPLIES, ETC.). SOLUTION COLOR IS MONITORED BY
A PHOTO-RESISTIVE OPTICAL SYSTEM WHICH WILL DETECT CHANGES AND ACTIVATE
AN ALARM CIRCUIT IF HARDNESS EXCEEDS THE SETPOINT. INCLUDES THE COLOR
CODED TEST REAGENTS, AND THE CONNECTION AND MOUNTI NG ACCESSORIES.
