CATHETOMETER. AN INSTRUMENT OF ACCURATE MEASUREMENT OF DISTANCES OR OF
THE SMALL DIFFERENCE OF DISTANCE OR HEIGHT SUCH AS THE UPPER SURFACE OF
TWO COLUMNS OF MERCURY OR OTHER FLUIDS OR OF THE SAME COLUMN AT
DIFFERENT TIMES. IT CONSISTS ESSENTIALLY OF A TELESCOPE, HORIZONTALLY
MOUNTED ON A CARRIAGE, WHICH TRAVELS ON AN ACCURATE VERTICAL AND/OR
HORIZONTAL GUIDE AND WITH AN ACCURATELY GRADUATED SCALE.
