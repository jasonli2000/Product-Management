WITH A LARGE MAGAZINE FOR HOLDING SENSITIZED PAPER, AND A PROCESSING

OR DRAWN, IN ACTUAL, REDUCED OR ENLARGED SIZE. PRINTS ARE MADE DIRECTLY
FROM THE ORIGINAL WITHOUT THE INTERVENTION OF A PLATE OR FILM NEGATIVE.
