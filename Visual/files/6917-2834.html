NOMENCLATURE 
SPECTROGRAPH. AN OPTICAL INSTRUMENT DESIGNED TO BREAK UP THE LIGHT FROM
A SOURCE INTO ITS CONSTITUENT WAVE LENGTHS AND TO PROVIDE A MEANS OF
QUALITATIVE OR QUANTITATIVE STUDY OF THE SPECTRUM LINE FORMED. THE
INSTRUMENT ESSENTIALY CONSISTS OF FOUR PARTS: THE SLIT, THE LENSES, THE
DISPERSION SYSTEM AND PHOTOGRAPHIC OR PHOTOELECTRIC SYSTEM. THE
INSTRUMENT MAY INCORPORATE A PRISM OR GRATING SYSTEM FOR THE DISPERSION
OF LIGHT.
