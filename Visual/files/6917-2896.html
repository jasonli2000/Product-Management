PLANIMETER, ELECTRONIC. AN INSTRUMENT DESIGNED FOR MEASURING THE AREA
AND/OR DISTANCE OF ANY PLANE SURFACE REPRESENTED BY FIGURES SUCH AS
PHOTOS, MAPS, PRINTS, DRAWINGS, DIAGRAMS, MICROSCOPIC IMAGE, AND THE
LIKE BY PASSING A TRACER OVER AND AROUND THE SURFACE. ITEM CONVERTS
GRAPHIC OBJECTS INTO NUMERICAL QUANTITY. ITEM MAY INCLUDE PRINTER,
