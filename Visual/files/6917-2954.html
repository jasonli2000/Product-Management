BRIEF DESCRIPTION DECT METAL
NOMENCLATURE 
DETECTOR, METAL. AN ELECTRONIC DEVICE DESIGNED TO DETECT CONCEALED OR
BURIED METAL OBJECTS SUCH AS METERS, PIPES, CONDUITS, CABLES, SEWER
SNAKES, SURGICAL INSTRUMENTS, AND THE LIKE. IT MAY DETECT METAL OBJECTS
IN VARIOUS TYPES OF ENVIRONMENTS SUCH AS WITHIN METAL CONTAINES; IN
SOILED LINEN BAGS, OR COINS BURRIED UNDERGROUND. IT MAY ALSO INCLUDE
PERSONNEL SCANNING AND A SCANNING OF SMALL PACKAGES. IT MAY EMIT AN
AUDIBLE NOISE, DISPLAY A PILOT LIGHT SIGNAL, 
 OR ACTIVATE A METER OR DIGITAL READOUT INDICATOR TO INDICATE A METAL
OBJECT HAS BEEN DETECTED. IT MAY INCLUDE A GAUGE FOR DETERMINING DEPTH
AND ANY OTHER ACCESSORY ITEMS.
