BRIEF DESCRIPTION TRAIN AID DENTAL CORR
NOMENCLATURE 
TRAINING AID, DENTAL, CORRECTIVE-PREVENTIVE TECHNIQUES. A VIDEO SYSTEM
DESIGNED TO TRAIN AND INSTRUCT A STUDENT OR PATIENT IN THE CORRECT
PROCEDURES OF CORRECTIVE-PREVENTIVE DENTAL TECHNIQUES FOR TOTALLY
REALISTIC SIMULATION OF ACTUAL PROCEDURES. LIVE BACTERIA OR PLAK IS
REMOVED FROM AREA(S) IN THE PATIENT'S MOUTH AND SLIDES PREPARED FOR
EXAMINATION UNDER THE PHASE MICROSCOPE. MAGNIFICATION OF SLIDES ARE
TRANSMITTED ONTO A PORTABLE SLOSED CIRCUIT T V WHERE THE ORAL
MICROBIATASTHEN CAN BE OBSERVED BY STUDENT(S) OR PATIENT(S). SYSTEM MAY
CONSIST OF PHASE MICROSCOPE, TV CAMERA, MONITOR, VIDEO-TAPE RECORDER,
AND STAND.
