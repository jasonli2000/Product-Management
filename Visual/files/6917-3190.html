CONNECTED TO A CONTROLLER WHICH, IN TURN, INTERFACES WITH T HE HOST
COMPUTER. IN ADDITION, TERMINALS MAY INCORPORATE CABLE TO OTHER
DEVICES, SUCH AS PRINTERS, MAGNETIC CARD READERS, ETC., AND MAY RESIDE
