BRIEF DESCRIPTION ENCODER & PROOF MACH
NOMENCLATURE 
ENCODER AND PROOF MACHINE. A MACHINE DESIGNED TO ENCODE (IMPRINT)
INFORMATION IN MAGNETIC INK ON DOCUMENTS FOR INPUT INTO DATA PROCESSING
SYSTEM. IMPRINTING IS MADE IN A SECTION OF A DOCUMENT LOCATED ALONG THE
BOTTOM MARGIN, AND THE SPACE IS SPECIFICALLY RESERVED FOR ACCEPTED
MAGNETIC INK IMPRINTING. IT MAY INCLUDE AN OPERATING CONSOLE, AN UPPER
INSTRUMENT CONSOLE, AN AUTOMATIC ENDORSING UNIT, AND SORT BINS FOR
EITHER ONEOR TWO CLASSIFICATIONS.
