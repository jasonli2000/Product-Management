BRIEF DESCRIPTION GOLF BALL PICKER
NOMENCLATURE 
GOLF BALL PICKER, VEHICLE DRAWN. A DEVICE DESIGNED FOR CLEARING THE
LAWN OF GOLF BALLS. THE GOLF BALLS ARE COLLECTED VIA CUSHION-WEDGE
PICK-UP BETWEEN THE SEMI-PNEUMATIC TIRES AND ARE DROPPED INTO A HOPPER.
IT IS DESIGNED TO BE PULLED BY A GOLF CART OR POWER MOWER, OR DESIGNED
TO BE PUSHED BY A VEHICLE AND MAY BE A SINGLE SECTION OR ATTACHED AS A
GANG.
