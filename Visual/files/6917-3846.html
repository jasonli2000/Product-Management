LOCKING DEVICE, SEMITRAILER, LOADING DOCK. A DEVICE FOR LOCKING A
SEMITRAILER TO THE LOADING DOCK. SEMITRAILERS ARE EQUIPPED WITH ICC
(INTERSTATE COMMERCE COMMISSION) BARS MOUNTED TO THE REAR OF THE
TRAILER,WHEN THE BAR IS CONNECTED TO THE LOCK IT BECOMES FIRMLY
ANCHORED TO THE BUILDING. IT PREVENTS ANY MOVEMENT OF THE TRAILER. MAY
BE COMPLETE WITH ON-OFF FLASHING LIGHTS SYSTEM.
