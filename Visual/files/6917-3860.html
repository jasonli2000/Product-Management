EMERGENCY/URGENT NOTIFICATION/RESPONSE SYSTEM. CONSISTS OF A MANAGER
SYSTEM AND A DELIVERY SYSTEM. THE MANAGER SYSTEM IS DESIGNED TO
CREATE/UPDATE CALL- LIST INDEPENDENTLY FROM THE DELIVERY SYSTEM. ON AN
EMERGENCY SITUATION, THE DELIVERY SYSTEM AUTOMATES THE NOTIFICATION
PROCESS BY THE USE OF A COMPUTER, TOUCH-TONE OR ROTARY TELEPHONE,
TOUCH-SENSITIVE SCREE, AN EXTERNAL SENSOR, OR BY A BARCODED READER AND 
 WILL DISPATCH/PROCESS THE CHOSEN MESSAGE(S). AS EACH CALL IS ANSWERED,
THE CALLER NEEDS TO CONFIRM HIS IDENTITY. SYSTEM MAY INCLUDE AN
EMERGENCY CELLULAR BACKUP SYSTEM.
