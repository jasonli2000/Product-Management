FOR LOW TO ULTRA-LOW TEMPERATURE COOLING BY MECHANICAL MEANS OR THROUGH
THE USE OF CRYOGENIC VAPOR OR LIQUID PHASE IMMERSION, WITH A RANGE THAT
FALLS BETWEEN 0 C AND -196 C. IT IS SPECIFICALLY FOR USE WHERE THE NEED
EXISTS FOR THE STORAGE OF BIOCHEMICALS, BIOLOGICALS, BACTERIOLOGICALS,
FROZEN BLOOD OR PLASMA, BONE MARROW, SEMEN, PHARMACEUTICALS, ETC. IT IS
ALSO SUITABLE FOR USE IN THE PRO CESSING OF BIOLOGICALS AND CHEMICALS
AND IN THE FIELD OF ULTRA-LOW TEMPERATURE FREEZING AND STORAGE
RESEARCH.
