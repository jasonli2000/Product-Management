DIALING DEVICE, TELEPHONE, AUTOMATIC. A DEVICE DESIGNED TO
AUTOMATICALLY DIAL OR REDIAL ANY ONE OF A QUANTITY OF TELEPHONE NUMBERS
THAT HAVE BEEN PROGRAMED INTO A COMPUTER SYSTEM, MAGNETIC TAPE, IBM
CARDS, OR LISTED ON AN ALPHA-NUMERIC INDEX AND THE LIKE. THIS DEVICE
MAY EITHER BE WIRED INTO THE TELEPHONE SET, OR JOINED TO IT BY USE OF A
SPECIAL JACK. WHEN EQUIPPED WITH A SELF-CONTAINED SPEAKER-PHONE, THE
ENTIRE PROCESS IS HANDSFREE, EXCEPT FOR DEPRESSING THE VARIOUS PUSH
BUTTON OR OTHER TYPE CONTROLS. MAY INCLUDE PROVISIONS FOR GROUP
CONFERENCE CALLS.
