SAW SETTING MACHINE (POWER). A POWER DRIVEN BENCH MOUNTED DEVICE
DESIGNED TO SET TEETH ON HAND SAW, CIRCULARSAW, AND/OR BAND SAW BLADES.

PLUNGER STRIKING AGAINST A SAW TOOTH, THE SAW TOOTH IS HELD OVER A
MATING ANVIL FOR SETTING THE POSITION OF SAW TEETH TO FORM A KERF WIDER
THAN THE THICKNESS OF THE SAW BLADE.
