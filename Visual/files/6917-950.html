BRIEF DESCRIPTION AMPLIFIER DIRECT CURR
NOMENCLATURE 
AMPLIFIER, DIRECT CURRENT. AN ELECTRONIC DEVICE WHOSE INPUT SIGNALS
CONTROL POWER FROM A SOURCE INDEPENDENT OF THE INPUT SIGNALS AND
DELIVERS OUTPUT SIGNALS OF GREATER AMPLITUDE WITH RESPECT TO CURRENT
AND/OR VOLTAGE. THE OUTPUT SIGNALS CHARACTERISTICS ARE UNIFORMLY
RELATED TO THE INPUT SIGNALS. IT IS USED FOR FOR SMALL VARIATIONS IN
DIRECT CURRENT (DC) ELECTRICAL IMPULSES, AND GENERALLY EMPLOYS DIRECT
COUPLING BETWEEN STAGES THROUGH RESISTORS.
