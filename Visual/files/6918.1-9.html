OTHER (9)    UTILITY SYSTEMS (6918.1)

Name Value
NAME OTHER
FULL NAME OTHER FACILITY SYSTEMS
DESCRIPTION 
Systems that are of interest in Facilities Management and not otherwise
classified. Examples may include:
 
       Laboratory Controls
       Remote Data Collection
       Traffic Management
       Automotive Systems
       Machine Controls
       Clock Systems
       Mail Handling Systems
       Microfilming
       Records Control
       Document Imaging
       Copiers
