GENERAL PRIVILEGE 
A.  General Denistry - qualified to diagnose, treat, and manage
patients with problems in dentistry according to usual, customary,
and reasonable methods and techniques, including extraction of
teeth and rehabilitation of dental arches.

by the emergency, even though not herein specified.
 
C.  Admit and discharge patients.
