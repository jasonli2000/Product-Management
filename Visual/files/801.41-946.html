This template is designed to help identify health problems that are
uniquely related to military service in Afghanistan and Iraq during
recent hazardous combat operations.  The questions target infectious
diseases, mental health problems, and chronic symptoms, which may 
develop in some veterans of Operation Enduring Freedom and Operation 
Iraqi Freedom.
