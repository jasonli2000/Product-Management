DIALOG/PROGRESS NOTE TEXT 
The VA National Center for Health Promotion and Disease Prevention 
(NCHPDP) and the US Preventive Services Task Force (USPSTF):
    * Suggests screening can safely be delayed until 3 years after onset
      of sexual activity or until age 21, whichever comes first
    * Recommends screening at least every 3 years
    * Suggests cervical cancer screening can be stopped after age 65
    * Recommends screening in older women who have not been previously
      screened, when information about previous screening is 
      unavailable, or when screening is unlikely to have occurred in  
      the past
