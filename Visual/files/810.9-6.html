NAME VA-HOMELESSNESS STOP CODES
DESCRIPTION 
The stop codes in this Reminder Location List are used as an exclusion
(AND NOT COHORT) to the VA-Homelessness Screening reminder. They are Stop
Codes indicating that the Veteran had a visit in the last 6 months with a 
provider assisting the Veteran with homelessness issues.
