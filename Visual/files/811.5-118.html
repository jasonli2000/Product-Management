DESCRIPTION 
This term will use the VA-WH BILATERAL MASTECTOMY taxonomy to find 
coded bilateral mastectomies. The health factor WH BILATERAL MASTECTOMY
distributed with this term may be used or add any local health factor that
represents the patient had a bilateral mastectomy and no longer needs
mammogram screening.
