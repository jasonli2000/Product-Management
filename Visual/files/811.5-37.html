NAME VA-IRAQ/AFGHAN SERVICE MONITOR
DESCRIPTION 
This term contains the health factor that is entered from the dialog if the 
patient did, in fact, serve in the combat arena (on the ground, in the air 
or at sea).
 
Service based on combat vet status is represented in a separate term for 
Combat Vet.
 
This term also contains computed findings for OEF/OIF service that 
evaluate service based on the patient file.
