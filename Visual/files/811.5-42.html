NAME VA-UNCONFIRMED IHD DIAGNOSIS
DATE CREATED 2001-10-17 00:00:00
DESCRIPTION 
This reminder term is used to represent an unconfirmed diagnosis of 
Ischemic Heart Disease on file.  The patients' medical record needs to 
have the IHD diagnosis removed from its source (PCE, PTF, or Problem 
List). The UNCONFIRMED IHD DIAGNOSIS health factor is pre-mapped to this 
reminder term upon distribution. Add local health factors your site may 
be using to represent this term.
