VA-BP >130/80 (ANY OF LAST 3) (43)    REMINDER TERM (811.5)

Name Value
NAME VA-BP >130/80 (ANY OF LAST 3)
DESCRIPTION 
This term looks at the last 3 blood pressures for any that are greater 
than 130/80.
FINDINGS
  1. FINDING ITEM:   BLOOD PRESSURE
    CONDITION:   I (V("SYSTOLIC")>130)!(V("DIASTOLIC")>80)
    OCCURRENCE COUNT:   3
    USE STATUS/COND IN SEARCH:   YES
    INTERNAL CONDITION:   I ($G(V("SYSTOLIC"))>130)!($G(V("DIASTOLIC"))>80)
    V SUBSCRIPT LIST:   "SYSTOLIC";"DIASTOLIC";
CLASS NATIONAL
SPONSOR VA National Center for Health Promotion and Disease Prevention (NCP)
