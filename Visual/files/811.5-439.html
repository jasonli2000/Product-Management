DESCRIPTION 
Use the WH PAP SMEAR SCREEN FREQ 1Y health factor distributed 
with this term or add any local health factor that represents the 
need for a yearly pap smear.
 
This term also contains the taxonomy for abnormal PAP/cervical cancer 
which will cause the PAP smear screening reminder to be due yearly unless 
some other frequency is specifically entered by the clinical staff.
