NAME VA-WH PAP SMEAR SCREEN NOT INDICATED
DATE CREATED 2003-07-21 00:00:00
DESCRIPTION 
Use the findings distributed with this reminder term or map any
local findings that indicate a PAP smear screen is not indicated
for this patient.  
 
This term is distributed with mapping to the following 
health factors:
     INACTIVATE CERVIX CANCER SCREEN (distributed with the
               first version of the Clinical Reminder package in 1996
               to inactivate the CERVICAL CANCER reminder).
     WH PAP SMEAR SCREEN NOT INDICATED
     VA LIMITED LIFE EXPECTANCY
and the following taxonomy: 
     VA-TERMINAL CANCER PATIENTS
 
Usage in National VA-WH PAP SMEAR SCREENING reminder:
 
This term is used in WH reminders to inactivate PAP Smear screening 
until a clinician indicates/selects frequency findings (4M, 6M, 1Y, 2Y
or 3Y) that override the inactivation.  This term is also used to
reactivate the PAP Smear screening reminder in the event the patient
flagged as a terminal cancer patient lives beyond a year or was flagged in
error.
 
 
Updated in patch PXRM*2.0*28 to include 2 new health factors which 
inactivate the screening reminder for 5 years:
 
WH CERV CA SCRN N/A 5 YRS-COMORBIDITIES
WH CERV CA SCRN N/A 5 YRS-LE<5YRS
