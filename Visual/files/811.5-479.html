VA-HOMELESSNESS - ALREADY RECEIVING SERVICES (479)    REMINDER TERM (811.5)

Name Value
NAME VA-HOMELESSNESS - ALREADY RECEIVING SERVICES
DESCRIPTION 
This Reminder Term is being used as an exclusion to homelessness 
screening. With a Beginning Date/Time set to 6M, if any finding that would
exclude the patient, is older than 6 Months, it is NOT found, and 
therefore, will NOT have an effect on the reminder cohort - will no longer
exclude the patient from the cohort. The RT contains:
 
1. A Reminder Location List ("VA-HOMELESSNESS STOP CODES") that includes
   all the Stop Codes indicative of a local visit in a VA Homelessness
   clinic. Stop Codes included: 504, 507, 508, 511, 522, 528, 529, 530, 
   590, 591, and 592.
 
2. A Health Factor ("ALREADY RECEIVING ASSIST WITH HOUSING") used in the
   dialog to indicate Veteran is receiving homelessness assistance from a
   source not captured by a local visit in a clinic with a Stop Code
   included in the Reminder Location List.
 
3. A Computed Finding ("VA-PROGRESS NOTE") which was actually entered 
   three times (X3). This was included to allow sites to enter one or more
   than one progress note title that is being used by local staff dealing
   with Veteran homelessness, but NOT in a clinic associated with a
   homelessness stop code. If Primary Care Staff, Social Workers or other
   staff are providing those services in a different stop code clinic,
   you may enter the note titles they use, and any of those titles will
   exclude the Veteran from screening for 6 months.
 
To enter titles to exclude the Veteran from screening, simply edit one of
the CF VA-PROGRESS NOTE Findings, and enter the EXACT Title in the 
"COMPUTED FINDING PARAMETER" field. If in the unlikely event that more 
than three (3) PN titles are in use locally for this purpose, enter that 
CF again as another finding, by typing "CF.VA-PROGRESS NOTE" (IN QUOTES), 
at the Finding: prompt.
