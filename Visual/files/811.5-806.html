NAME VA-COGNITIVE IMPAIRMENT
DESCRIPTION 
Patients unable to answer questions related to screening tools may be 
excluded from the denominator if the cognitive impairment is well 
documented.

cognitive impairment.  In order to be excluded from the cohort for EPRP,
