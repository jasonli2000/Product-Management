NAME VA-CHRONIC MEDICAL CONDITION
DESCRIPTION 
Screening for depression may not be possible in patients with some chronic
medical conditions.  This term represents any data element that is used 
to indicate that the patient has an chronic medical condition that
prevents screening for depression.  E.g. severe dementia.
