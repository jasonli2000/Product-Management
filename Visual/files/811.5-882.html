VA-MH KEPT APPTS FOR MHTC ASSIGNMENT (882)    REMINDER TERM (811.5)

Name Value
NAME VA-MH KEPT APPTS FOR MHTC ASSIGNMENT
DESCRIPTION 
This reminder term is used to identify MH appointments that were kept by
the patient, where the appointment is checked out and a completed 
encounter has been documented.  The Reminder Location List of clinics used
by this term was specified by the Office of Mental Health Services.
FINDINGS
  1. FINDING ITEM:   VA-APPOINTMENTS FOR A PATIENT
    CONDITION:   I +V("OUTPATIENT ENCOUNTER IEN")>0!(+V("CHECK-OUT DATE/TIME")>0)
    INTERNAL CONDITION:   I +$G(V("OUTPATIENT ENCOUNTER IEN"))>0!(+$G(V("CHECK-OUT DATE/TIME"))>0)
    V SUBSCRIPT LIST:   "OUTPATIENT ENCOUNTER IEN";"CHECK-OUT DATE/TIME";
    COMPUTED FINDING PARAMETER:   FLDS:1,2,3,11,12^STATUS:R^LL:VA-MHTC APPT STOP CODES LL
CLASS NATIONAL
SPONSOR Office of Mental Health Services
