Name Value
NAME VA-OEF/OIF MONITOR REPORTING
PRINT NAME OEF/OIF POST-DEPLOYMENT REPORT
DESCRIPTION 
COHORT: veterans with separation date after 9/11/01, who served in 
OEF/OIF  or whose service is not known and who have had 2 visits to a
NEXUS clinic after the most recent service separation date and at least 
one visit in the past 1 year.
 
Patients with an entry of NO IRAQ/AFGHAN SERVICE are excluded from the 
cohort.
 
RESOLUTION: If the veteran served in Iraq or Afghanistan (IRAQ/AFGHAN
SERVICE) then
 
all the other items are required to resolve the reminder
and must be completed after the date of the most recent service
separation or within 120 days of the most recent service separation date:
  a) screen for PTSD,
  b) screen for depression,
  c) screen for alcohol use,
  d) all 3 screening questions related to infectious diseases.
 
Refusals are valid for one year for alcohol, PTSD and depression.  Since 
these are yearly screens, these should be addressed at least on a yearly 
basis using the individual reminders for these screens.  Refusals entered 
prior to service separation are ignored by this reminder.
