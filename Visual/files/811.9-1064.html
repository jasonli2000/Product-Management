VA-CV ELIG W/HF FOR NO SERVICE (1064)    REMINDER DEFINITION (811.9)

Name Value
NAME VA-CV ELIG W/HF FOR NO SERVICE
PRINT NAME CV ELIGIBLE - PT INDICATES NO SVC
DESCRIPTION 
This reminder is designed to aid sites in identifying patients whose 
combat veteran eligibility data and data collected from the OEF/OIF 
Screening reminder are not in agreement.
 
This reminder is due for patients who CV status is eligible or expired, 
whose LSSD is >9/11/01 but who have an entry of NO IRAQ/AFGHAN SERVICE.
 
The date last done represents that date that the health factor for no 
OEF/OIF service was entered.
