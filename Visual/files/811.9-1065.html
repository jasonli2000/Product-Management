Name Value
NAME VA-CV INELG W/HF FOR SERVICE
PRINT NAME CV INELIGIBLE - PT INDICATES SVC
DESCRIPTION 
This reminder is designed to aid sites in identifying patients whose
combat veteran eligibility data and data collected from the OEF/OIF
Screening reminder are not in agreement.
 
This reminder is due for patients who CV status is not eligible,
whose LSSD is >9/11/01 but who have an entry of IRAQ/AFGHAN SERVICE.
 
The date last done represents that date that the health factor for OEF/OIF
service was entered.
