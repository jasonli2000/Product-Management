VA-LIPID STATIN RX CVD/DM (VER1.0) (1539)    REMINDER DEFINITION (811.9)

Name Value
NAME VA-LIPID STATIN RX CVD/DM (VER1.0)
PRINT NAME Assess Statin Use - Lipids (CVD/DM)
DESCRIPTION 
Applicable to diabetics and patients with CVD ages 18-75 who have had an 
LDL in the past 2 years.
 
Resolved by LDL<100, moderate or high dose statin, contraindication to
statins, other provider actions/documentation.
 
Frequency: set at 99 years (although this is somewhat arbitrary since the 
reminder would work just as well with this set to anything 2Y or more).
 
The age range is set by using 2 function findings so that if there is a 
decision to use different age ranges for the different diagnoses, then 
this functionality is already available in the reminder.
TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION 
Cohort logic (inclusion/exclusion criteria):
 
1.  Diabetes - 2 diagnoses in the past 2 years, or a problem list 
entry, or a prescription of medication for diabetes that was active in the
previous 9 months and the diagnosis or prescription is more recent than
any entry of an incorrect diagnosis (FF4).  And age is 50-75.
 
(((FI(VA-DIABETES HEDIS)&FF(1))!FI(VA-DIABETES HEDIS PROB 
LIST)!FI(VA-DIABETES MEDICATIONS))&FF(4)&FI(VA-LDL DIABETES AGE RANGE))
 
 
2.  CVD - any diagnosis of CVD in the past 2 years that is more 
recent than any entry of incorrect diagnosis of CVD and age 50-75.  
 
!(FI(VA-IHD AND ASVD)&FF(6)&FI(VA-LDL IHD/ASCVD AGE RANGE))
 
 
3.  Any entry of high risk for CVD entered as a health factor and 
linked to the CHD RISK>20% reminder term.  Sites that use a CHD 
calculator may have these patients designated and may want to include 
them in this lipid management reminder. 
 
!(FI(VA-CHD RISK >20%)&FF(8))
 
 
4. Exclude those with limited life expectancy - a national reminder term 
already exists for this.
 
&'FI(VA-LIFE EXPECTANCY <6 MONTHS)
 
 
The age range is set by using 2 function findings so that if there is a 
decision to use different age ranges for the different diagnoses, then 
this functionality is already available in the reminder and these ranges 
can be adjusted locally if needed.
 
 
5. Require an LDL done in the past 2 years - if not done, then the local 
site should have a reminder for doing a lipid panel on a regular basis 
and that needs to be done first.  Two years was chosen so that a recent 
lipid panel that was done but not done in the past 12 months, would still 
trigger the thought and assessment about statin use since this is the 
most important intervention and treating to goal is less critical.
 
 
Resolution logic:
1.    LDL - LDL<100 at the VA or LDL <100 outside the VA and this is 
         the most recent value (FF13)
         Compare date of FI(13) or FI(14) to any prior LDL that was over 
             99 (Findings 15,17,18,19)
 
2.   Statin
  a.    High dose statin tablets prescribed - VA prescription FI(10).
  b.    Moderate dose statin - VA prescription for a moderate dose and 
         the tablets are not being split (FF11 compares the days supply to
         the quantity supplied and if there is a large discrepancy, tablet
         splitting is assumed.  FI(11)&FF(11).
  c.    Non-VA statin and the verification by staff using a health factor 
          that this is a moderate or high dose of a statin since the dose
          of non-VA meds could not be evaluated by the reminder.  
          FI(27)&FI(24).
 
3.   Other action or documentation
  a.    FI(20): Add statin or adjust dose health factors, 2 months.
  b.    FI(21): Patient refusal, 2 months.
  c.    FI(22): Documentation of non-adherence, 2 months.
  d.    FI(23): Documentation that the patient is already on the highest
           tolerated dose, 1 year.
  e.    FI(25): Order for repeat LDL, T-14 days to T+ 2 months.
  f.    FI(26): Entry of a health factor indicating that a lipid profile 
           was ordered, 1 month.
  g.    (FI(29)&(FI(28)!FF(29))): Entry of a health factor indicating ADR 
           to all available statins FI(29) entered in the past 1 year
           (FF29) or the health factor plus an actual entry in the
           allergy/ADR list (FI28) ever. 
  h.    FI(30): Temporary contraindication to a statin (pregnancy),
           1 year.
