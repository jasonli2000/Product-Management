NAME VA-PTSD REASSESSMENT (PCL)
PRINT NAME PCL Needed for PTSD
DO IN ADVANCE TIME FRAME 30D
DESCRIPTION 
Reminder designed by the Office of Mental Health to provide a way to
measure the outcomes of Veterans being treated for PTSD.
 
Reminder applicable to OEF/OIF service veterans who are in active 
treatment for PTSD which is defined as 2 visits to a Mental Health clinic 
in the past 120 days + 2 primary diagnoses of PTSD at an outpatient visit 
in the past 120 days.
 
Not applicable to those who are severely cognitively impaired or those 
who are no longer in active treatment for PTSD.
 
Resolved by completion of the items in the PCL-S.  Since the PCL-S is not 
yet available in the MHA software, a PCL-C plus the questions on what and 
when the trauma occurred and the impact on the patients are included in 
the dialog until such time that the PCL-S can be made available.
