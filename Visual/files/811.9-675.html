SEX SPECIFIC MALE
DESCRIPTION 
This reminder is used for branching logic in the VA-ALCOHOL AUDIT-C 
POSITIVE F/U EVAL reminder to determine if the patient might be drinking 
within safe limits. 
 
Some patients with an AUDIT-C of 5 can still be drinking within safe 
limits and so these patients should be asked specifically if they drink 
within safe limits.  This reminder is DUE NOW for those patients and the 
question is presented in the reminder dialog.
