NAME VA-ALCOHOL AUDIT-C POSITIVE F/U EVAL
PRINT NAME Positive AUDIT-C Needs Evaluation
DESCRIPTION 
This reminder is due for patients who have a Positive Alcohol Use 
Screen.  It becomes due with an AUDIT-C score of 5 or more.
 
This setting of AUDIT-C of 5 or higher is for the national performance 
measure.  There are still patients who have lower scores who drink above 
the safe limit and these patients should also be counseled.
 
The dialog includes assessment tools and documentation of advice,
counseling and referral
 
It resolves by documentation of direct alcohol related advice and 
education about alcohol related medical problems.  Options for further 
assessment and referral are also included.
 
It is intended for use by providers.
 
Update March 2009

2. Make advice and feedback to the patient consistent and not forced.

same visit.
TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION 
This reminder is triggered by the AUDIT-C score generated from the
ALCOHOL USE SCREEN clinical reminder.  It uses branching logic and 
will display different screens depending on the AUDIT-C score.  A score 
of 8 or more will display a screen geared towards patients with a higher 
risk of alcohol abuse or dependence.  A score of less than 8 will display 
a screen to guide counseling and assessment for those with a lower risk 
of dependence.

GENERAL COHORT FOUND TEXT 
Patients with no prior history of alcohol related problems or prior
treatment for alcohol abuse or dependence who have an AUDIT-C score of
4-7 (score of 3-7 for women) should be advised to stay within recommended
drinking limits.
 
RECOMMENDED LIMITS:
    Men:   <= 14 drinks/wk and maximum of 4 drinks/occasion
    Women: <=  7 drinks/wk and maximum of 3 drinks/occasion
 
Patients with prior alcohol problems and ANY positive AUDIT-C score should
be considered for referral to a Substance Abuse program.  These patients
are at high risk of dependence.
 
Standard drinks:   \\
     0.5 fluid ounces of absolute alcohol  \\
     12 ounces of beer                      \\
     5 ounces of wine                       \\
     1.5 ounces of 80-proof spirits         \\
