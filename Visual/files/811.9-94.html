LONG TERM STEROID USE (94)    REMINDER DEFINITION (811.9)

Name Value
NAME LONG TERM STEROID USE
PRINT NAME Long Term Steroid Use
DESCRIPTION 
Used to identify patients on long term steroids that put them at risk of 
pneumococcal disease.  Per CDC, those patients should receive PCV13 
vaccination.
 
This reminder is sent out set to look for 3 prescriptions for steroids 
  the most recent one with a days supply of >21 and 2 prior prescriptions 
with durations of >21 days.  Current prescriptions could have a very 
short duration since the duration is the start date to today for an 
active prescription.  This is why for the most recent Rx, the search is 
for days supply and not for duration.
 
The reminder also looks for any recent Rx that has a days supply of >70 
days - and counts this as long term steroid use also.
