DEFINITION 
Bariatric medicine is the medical treatment of obesity and its associated 
conditions.  Bariatric medicine addresses the obese individual with a 
comprehensive program of diet, exercise and behavior modification, and 
when indicated, the prescription of appropriate medications as determined 
by the Bariatric physician.  Bariatric physicians are licensed physicians 
who offer specialized programs in the medical treatment of obesity and 
its associated conditions. 
 
Source:  American Society of Bariatric Physicians [7/1/2007: new]
