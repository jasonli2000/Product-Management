DEFINITION 
An Internist who practices Sleep Medicine is certified in the 
subspecialty of sleep medicine and specializes in the clinical 
assessment, physiologic testing, diagnosis, management and prevention of 
sleep and circadian rhythm disorders. Sleep specialists treat patients of 
any age and use multidisciplinary approaches. Disorders managed by sleep 
specialists include, but are not limited to, sleep related breathing 
disorders, insomnia, hypersomnias, circadian rhythm sleep disorders, 
parasomnias and sleep related movement disorders.
 
Source: American Academy of Sleep Medicine [7/1/2006: new]
