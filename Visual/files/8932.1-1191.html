DEFINITION 
An internist with special knowledge and the skill required of a 
gastroenterologist to care for patients prior to and following hepatic 
transplantation that spans all phases of liver transplantation. Selection 
of appropriate recipients requires assessment by a team having experience 
in evaluating the severity and prognosis of patients with liver disease.
