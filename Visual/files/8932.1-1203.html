SPECIALTY CODE 86
DEFINITION 
A neurologist or child neurologist who specializes in the diagnosis and 
management of disorders of nerve, muscle or neuromuscular junction, 
including amyotrophic lateral sclerosis, peripheral neuropathies (e.g., 
diabetic and immune mediated neuropathies), various muscular dystrophies, 
congenital and acquired myopathies, inflammatory myopathies (e.g., 
polymyositis, inclusion body myositis) and neuromuscular transmission 
disorders (e.g., myasthenia gravis, Lambert-Eaton myasthenic syndrome).
