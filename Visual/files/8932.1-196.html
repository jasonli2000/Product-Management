DEFINITION 
Chiropractic Neurology is defined as the field of functional neurology 
that engages the internal - and external environment of the individual in 
a structured and targeted approach to affect positive changes in the 
nervous system and consequently the physiology and behavior of an 
individual. Chiropractic Neurologists are board-certified specialists in 
non-drug, non-surgical care for those with neurologically based health 
problems. There are many conditions people suffer from that are in this 
broad category: learning and attention disorders, headaches, vertigo, 
pain syndromes, developmental disorders, nerve injury, spinal cord 
injury, head injury or stroke, movement disorders, and many other 
conditions. 
 
Source: American Chiropractic Neurology Board, 2008 & American 
Chiropractic Association, 2008
