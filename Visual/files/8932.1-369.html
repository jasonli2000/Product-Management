DEFINITION 
A social worker is a person who is qualified by a Social Work degree, and 
licensed, certified or registered by the state as a social worker to 
practice within the scope of that license. A social worker provides 
assistance and counseling to clients and their families who are dealing 
with social, emotional and environmental problems. Social work services 
may be rendered to individuals, families, groups, and the public.
 
Source: National Association of Social Workers, 2009
