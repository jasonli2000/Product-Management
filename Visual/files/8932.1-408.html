DEFINITION 
Music therapists use music interventions to assess clients' strengths and 
needs, develop goals, implement services, and evaluate and document 
progress for individuals of all ages. Music therapists facilitate changes 
in physical, cognitive, emotional, and/or psychosocial health.
Source: American Music Therapy Association
