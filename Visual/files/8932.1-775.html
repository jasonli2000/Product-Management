X12 CODE 207RA0401X
SPECIALTY CODE 11
DEFINITION 
An internist doctor of osteopathy that specializes in the treatment of 
addiction disorders. A doctor of osteopathy that is board 
eligible/certified by the American Osteopathic Board of Internal Medicine 
can obtain a Certificate of Added Qualifications in the field of 
Addiction Medicine.
 
Source: American Osteopathic Board of Internal Medicine, 2007.
