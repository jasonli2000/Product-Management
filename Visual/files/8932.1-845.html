DEFINITION 
A developmental-behavioral specialist is a pediatrician with special 
training and experience who aims to foster understanding and promotion of 
optimal development of children and families through research, education, 
clinical care and advocacy efforts. This physician assists in the 
prevention, diagnosis, and management of developmental difficulties and 
problematic behaviors in children and in the family dysfunctions that 
compromise children's development.
