NAME XUDOC SPOOL USE5
HEADER MAKING SPOOL DOCUMENTS INTO MAIL MESSAGES
TEXT 
This option provides a way to use your spooled document just as you would
use a mail message.  All the MailMan functions would be available.  You
could copy and edit the text.  You could forward the message locally or
across the network.  If you use this option, take care not to abuse the
privilege.  Keep the number and size of your documents down to a minimum.
 
When using this option, pick the spool document that you would like
to make into a mail message and it will be instantly transformed!  Now go
to your MailMan Menu and 'Read' your IN basket mailbox.  Scan the most
recently delivered ones to find your spool document now as a mail message.
 
Note: Contact your IRM representative if you are not able to use this
option; special access privileges are required.
