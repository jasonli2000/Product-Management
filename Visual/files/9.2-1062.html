NAME XM-U-P-SURROGATE WRITE PRIV
HEADER SURROGATES - WRITE PRIVILEGES
TEXT 
 
Users who give surrogates 'Read and Write' privileges, in addition to all
the privileges of [READ] access, allow the surrogates to do any of the
following:
 
  * [ANSWER] messages.
 
  * [COPY] messages.
 
  * [EDIT] messages.
 
  * [SEND] messages.
 
  * [WRITE] messages.
