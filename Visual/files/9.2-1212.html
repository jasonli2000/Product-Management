                           Working with Lists
 
Being able to find outstanding problems from a site, package, and
specialist are just a few of the uses for lists.  You can review calls
from any NOIS list.  You cant change the definition of others lists or
store calls on their lists.  You can define your own custom lists.  Youll
find that as you use lists its easy to find calls of interest without
having to define criteria or store calls.  Usually one or two personal
lists will do.
