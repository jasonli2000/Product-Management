                             Making Notes
 
Notes can be made to add comments concerning the progress of the call.
 
Notes do not change the status of the call.  A note does change the last
time the call was edited (of interest when specifying notification).
 
Notes are appended to an ongoing word-processing field that contains all
notes.
 
Once a note has been entered, it cannot be re-edited (somewhat like
replying to a mail message).  You can make additional notes, but not edit
those that have been entered.
 
All notes are viewed on a detailed display.  The brief format only
displays the first three lines of the last note entered.
