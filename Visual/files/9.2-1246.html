NAME FSC U1104 AUTO NOTIFICATION
TEXT 
 
                        Automatic Notification
 
You can be automatically notified when calls are edited or the status
changes.
 
To do this, you use a list that you own.
You can specify an event method - mail or alert, and event - edit or
status change.
 
Whenever calls are updated to your list, the calls are scheduled for
notification.  Mail uses an email address in the NOIS Specialist file.
 
Since the list is defined with a query, any criteria can be used for your
notification.
