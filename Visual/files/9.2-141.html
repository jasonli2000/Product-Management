NAME XQTRUN
HEADER Invoking a Menu Template
TEXT 
   Menu templates are activated by typing a left, square bracket
followed by the name you have assigned to the template.  For example,
to invoke a menu template called CHECK, you would enter [[CHECK.
 
   Select Menu option: [[CHECK
                       ------
 
   After the list of options in the template has been run, you will see
the prompt: "Run CHECK Again? {Y/N} Y/" and if you answer "Y" or simply
<RETURN> (since "Y" is the default) the template will be executed again.
If you answer "N" you will be returned to your regular menus at the point
where you invoked the template.
