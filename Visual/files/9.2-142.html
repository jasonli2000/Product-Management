NAME XQTNAM
HEADER Naming a Menu Template
TEXT 
 
   To name your new Menu Template simply choose a short name (6 characters or
less) that you will remember.  If you forget the name you can list your
templates and it will show you all that you have.  A template name may not
include the characters "^" or "?".  It must be composed of alphbetic and/or
numeric characters, A to Z and 0 to 9.
   A Menu Template might be named "MAIL", for instance, if it navigates to the
MailMan options.  Or "REG" if it registers a patient.  It can be almost
anything that you choose.
