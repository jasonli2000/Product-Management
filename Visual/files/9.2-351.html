                         Time Range
 
The start and end dates determine the time range for the report.  The 
date range can uses the dates entered.  You can also enter months. 
For example 1/93 to 3/93 will report all data for those three months.
A date range of 1/14/93 to 3/15/93 does not include all three months.
 
The report uses the time range to restrict the data to those specimen 
collection dates within the time range.
 
The dates entered determine a time range.  It does not matter if the start 
date is earlier or later than the end date.  The time range applies to all 
types of reports selected.
