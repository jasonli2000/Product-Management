                        Detailed Reports
 
A detailed report can be included in the antibiotic trend report.  In 
addition to totals of susceptibilities, this report includes information on 
each isolate.  The patient, antibiotic interpretations, accession, 
collection date, a flag if the isolate was merged with a previous isolate, 
and the length of stay since the collection date.
 
This report can be used to confirm the percentage of susceptibility and 
counts of organisms.  It can also be used as a survey tool, providing more 
detailed information to track areas of interest.
 
A detailed report does not take much longer to process than a non-detailed    
report but it does print out many more pages of information.
