NAME OR PATIENT LOOKUP
HEADER Enhanced Patient Look-up
TEXT 
 
You may wish to choose patients who do not appear on the CURRENT LIST, or
to change the list from which selections are to be made.  Either of
these may be accomplished by:
                    
      PATIENT:  You may select an INDIVIDUAL patient by entering his/her
                NAME, SSN, ROOM/BED, or ["LAST INITIAL_LAST FOUR"]
                at the Select Patient(s): prompt.
  
         LIST:  You may modify the list from which you are selecting
                by entering a [LOCATION], [TREATING SPECIALTY],
                [PROVIDER], [PERSON] or [PATIENT LIST] name at the
                Select Patient(s): prompt.  The [PRECEDENCE] in which
                these are searched may effect the list which is retrieved.
 
                To limit the search to a Treating Specialty, Provider/Person
                or Patient List, you may use the following optional syntax:
                     S.<specialty name>,
                     P.<provider or person name> or
                     L.<list name>
                respectively.
