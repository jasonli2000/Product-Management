TEXT 
 
Consistent with the existing DHCP convention you may enter the Patient's
Last Initial followed by the last four digits of his/her SSN to unambig-
uously select a given patient.  For example, if you wish to select William
C. Doe, whose DOB is SEP 12, 1944 and whose SSN is 234236572, you enter:
 
                                  1A ward list
 
1     DOE,WILLIAM C. (6572)             7     NIVEK,BETA (3242)
2     FUNOF,IT (9876)                   8     NIVEK,GAMMA (4324)
3     MARLEY,JACOB (5678)               9     NIVEK,SIGMA (2379)
4     MUFFET,LITTELLA M (7689)          10    SPRAT,JACK (2342)
5     NEW,PATIENT (1234)                11    TURNER,TOMMY (2342)
6     NIVEK,ALPHA (8796)
 
Select Patient(s): D6572
 
The program will respond with:
 
Select Patient(s): D6572  DOE,WILLIAM C.      09-12-44   234236572
DATE ENTERED 1991-09-11 15:37:00
