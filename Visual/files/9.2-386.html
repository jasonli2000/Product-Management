NAME OR PERSON
HEADER Personal Patient List Look-up
TEXT 
 
Entering all or part of a valid (non-provider) Person's name at the 
prompt Select Patient(s) will cause the list to be rebuilt to include
the patients in that person's PRIMARY patient list, and presented to
you anew.  For example, if you wish to choose from all patient's
in Mr. Bill Smallpotatoe's primary patient list, you may enter:
 
                          METHUSELA,O.C. patient list
 
1     DOE,WILLIAM C. (6572)             5     MUFFET,LITTELLA M (7689)
2     ESSTEPON,GLORD (3234)             6     SPRAT,JACK (2342)
3     KAPLON,DENNIS (3242)              7     TURNER,TOMMY (2342)
4     MARLEY,JACOB (5678)
 
Select Patient(s): SMALLPOTATOES,WILLIAM A.
The current patient list will be cleared.
