Name Value
NAME OR PATIENT LIST
HEADER Patient List Look-up
TEXT 
 
Entering all or part of a valid PATIENT LIST name at the prompt to
Select Patient(s) will cause the list to be rebuilt to include
the patients in that patient list, and presented to you anew.
For example, if you wish to choose from all patient's in the list
named TEAM 7B you could enter:
 
                             BILL'S 1A patient list
 
1     BLANDRU,AB (3333)                 5     NIVEK,BETA (3242)
2     MARLEY,JACOB (5678)               6     NIVEK,GAMMA (4324)
3     NEW,PATIENT (1234)                7     NIVEK,SIGMA (2379)
4     NIVEK,ALPHA (8796)
 
Select Patient(s): TEAM
     1   TEAM 1A
     2   TEAM 2B
     3   TEAM 7B
CHOOSE 1-3: 3  
The current patient list will be cleared.
