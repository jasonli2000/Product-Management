Consider the following patients:
 
1.   SMITH,JOE                   222-33-4444
2.   SMITH,JANE                  222-44-5555
3.   SMYTHE,ALICE                555-33-4141
4.   JONES,JAMES                 222-33-4141
 
To identify #1, you could enter: 
   4444 - this would uniquely identify the patient
   S4444 - would also uniquely identify and be much quicker
   SMITH - would match JOE and JANE and you be prompted for a choice.
 
To identify #3, you could enter:
   4141 - this would match ALICE and JAMES
   S4141 - would uniquely identify ALICE
   SMY - this would also uniquely identify ALICE
