NAME PRCS1358
HEADER New 1358 Request (Section)
TEXT 
Use a 1358 Order request to budget money for ongoing service 
expenses, such as the utility bill, copier repair, rent, or postage. A 1358 
Order allows the Control Point to "obligate funds," or establish a budget 
for ongoing services, so there will be money to pay the vendor when the 
monthly or quarterly statement is due.
