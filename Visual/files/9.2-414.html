                EDIT PHARMACY INTERVENTION
 
 
The EDIT PHARMACY INTERVENTION option is used to edit any 
existing Pharmacy Interventions.  The option appears on the INTM 
(Pharmacy Intervention Menu) menu as:
 
        EINT   Edit Pharmacy Intervention
 
You can choose this option by entering EINT at the
 
        Select Pharmacy Intervention Menu option:
 
prompt.
 
You are first asked to enter the intervention that you wish to edit.  You may 
enter the date and then select from the choices shown (if more than one 
entered on the date selected) or you may enter the patient's chart number 
which is the prefered method.  Once you have slected the intervention to edit 
you may edit any of the fields in the manner you are used to.  You may also an
up arrow with the field name if you want to go directly to that field.  The 
following is an example of selecting the correct intervention by both entering 
the date and then by entering the patient's name.
