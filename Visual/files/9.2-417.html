                VIEW INTERVENTION
 
The VIEW INTERVENTION option is used to view one or more 
interventions in a captioned format.  The option appears on the INTM 
(Pharmacy Intervention Menu) menu as:
 
          VINT   View Intervention
 
You can choose this option by entering VINT at the
 
        Select Pharmacy Intervention Menu option:
 
prompt.
 
You will first be asked which intervention you wish to view.  You may either 
enter the date of the intervention and choose the correct one when listed or 
you may enter the patient's chart number or name.  The chart number or 
name would be the prefered method.  You may keep entering another one, 
then when you have entered all the ones you wish to view just enter a return at 
the 'Another One' prompt.
