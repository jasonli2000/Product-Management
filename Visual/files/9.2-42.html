Name Value
NAME XQHELP-ASSIGNEDITOR
HEADER ASSIGNING AND DE-ASSIGNING EDITORS
TEXT 
These options are used to assign editors of help frames.
The assignment of frames can be done in groups, or individually
You will be prompted to list frames and users.  You may assign
a single frame to a group of users, or multiple frames to an individual user
or multiple frames to a group of users.
 
You will be prompted for the [HELP FRAME] and the [EDITOR].
Specify each, one at a time, terminating the last with a <return>.
For additional help on these prompts type '?'.
 
When done specifying the lists, you will be shown those frames and users
you have selected and you will be asked if you wish to proceed.  If you
respond with a 'no' you may go back and edit your selection.  Otherwise
the frames will then be assigned to the users specified. 
 
You may 'de-select' a frame or user by prefacing it with a '-'.  You can
also use a MAIL GROUP to select a group of users.
