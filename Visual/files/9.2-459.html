GMRG-DELETING ENTRIES (459)    HELP FRAME (9.2)

Name Value
NAME GMRG-DELETING ENTRIES
HEADER Deleting Selections
TEXT 
To delete a previously selected entry from the patient data, enter a
selection number, or range of selections separated by a hyphen (-), and 
then enter an '@' afterwards.
 
   E.g.    1@ - deletes selection 1.
           1@,3@ - deletes selections 1 and 3.
           1-2@ - deletes selections 1 and 2.
           2@,4-5@ - deletes selections 2, 4 and 5.
 
NOTE:  Data is not actually being deleted because an audit trail has been
       established to track the patient data at any point in time.
