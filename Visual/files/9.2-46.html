NAME XQHELP-MOREOPT
HEADER MORE OPTIONS IN THE HELP PROCESSOR
TEXT 
The Help Processor also includes the following options:
 
[ASSIGN EDITORS] -      Allows the author of a help frame to assign editors
                        to one or more frames.
 
[DE-ASSIGN EDITORS] -   Allows the author of a frame to take away editorship
                        privileges for one or more frames.
 
[NEW/REVISED FRAMES] -  Produces a listing of frames by package, and date
                        last updated.
