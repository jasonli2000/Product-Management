TEXT 
To add/append text to clarify an entry:
 
   1.  Enter the entry number, or range of entries separated by a hypen (-),
       and enter a ';' afterwards.
 
        E.g.   1;  or  1;,3;  or  1-3;  or  2;,4-6;
 
   2.  After this an enter/edit the 'appended text' prompt for each selected
       entry will be displayed at which time you may enter/edit appended
       text.
 
OR if you are only appending text to one entry, you may type the
   entry number followed by a ';' and the text you wish to append.
 
      E.g.  1;further information,3;additional clarification
