HEADER Bracketed Text
TEXT 
To edit/add text within the square brackets for an entry:
 
  1.  Enter the entry number, or a range of entries separated by a hyphen (-),
      and enter a '/' afterward.
 
         E.g.   1/  or  1/,3/  or  3-4/  or  2/,6-8/
 
  2.  For each of the entries you have selected, you will be asked to
      edit/add the text within the square brackets.
 
OR  if there is only one set of square brackets for an entry containing
    bracketed text that you wish to edit, you may enter the entry number
    followed by a '/' and the text you wish to insert in the brackets.
 
    E.g.  1/clarifier,4/text to clarify
 
 
REMEMBER:  If you select an entry with bracketed text, and do not edit the
           text you will be given the default value.
