NAME GMRG-SELECTIONS
HEADER Making Selections
TEXT 
When prompted to select the numbers of the desired entry(ies)
you may do the following:
  1.  [ADD] the entry to the patient data.
  2.  [DELETE] the entry from the patient data.
  3.  [APPEND] text to the entry to further clarify the entry for
      the patient.
  4.  If the entry has square brackets in it, you can edit the
      [INTERNAL TEXT] of the entry.
  5.  Type an 'A' if 'Additional Text: ' appears at the end of the
      selection list to add additional text for the term.
  6.  OR you can do a [COMBINATION] of any of the above options.
