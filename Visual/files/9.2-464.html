NAME GMRG-COMBINED ENTRY
HEADER Combined Selections
TEXT 
The following are examples of some combined options that can be executed
at the selection prompt:
 
  E.g.   1;text,4-5  will add selections 4, 5 and append 'text' to
                     selection 1 (if selection 1 was not previously
                     chosen this will also add selection 1).
         2-3@,4/     will delete selections 2, 3 and allow you to
                     edit the bracketed text of selection 4 (if selection
                     4 was not previously chosen this will also add
                     selection 4).
         2/text,3;/  will insert 'text' between the brackets for
                     selection 2, allow appending of text and editing
                     of bracketed text for selection 3.  Also these
                     entries will be added if not previously chosen.
         1-3,A       will add selections 1, 2, 3 and additional text
                     for this term.
