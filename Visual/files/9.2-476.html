TEXT 
At the prompt Select UNION LIST OF PERIODICALS TITLE, it is
recommended that you use one or more of these methods to
find the correct title:
 
   (1) a [SIGNIFICANT] word or word fragment
   (2) the [ISSN] or ISBN number
   (3) the NLM title abbreviation
   (4) the first few words of a title, [TRUNCATING] from the right
   (5) a [SYNONYM] for the title
 
Many titles have variant forms; please see [HINTS] for tips on
searching for them.  See [DISPLAY] for an example of the bibliographic
record.
