At the prompt Select VALNET LOCATION, enter the first part of a
VALNET library's five-letter code.  You will then be presented
with that library's fax number(s), region and district numbers,
and mailing address.  For example:
 
Select VALNET LOCATION: <MET><CR>
 
LIBRARY: METOG,TOGUS
FAX NUMBER: 207/623-2475 COMM
REGION:    1
DISTRICT:  1
 
NAME:         Library Service (142D)
ADDRESS:      Veterans Administration
              VA Medical and Regional Office Center
              TOGUS, ME  04330
