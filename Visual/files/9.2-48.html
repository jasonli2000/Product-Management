HEADER HOW TO BUILD AND EDIT HELP FRAMES
TEXT 
On entering a help frame, you will be prompted for the following fields
in the help frame file:
NAME, HEADER, TEXT, and RELATED FRAMES.
The AUTHOR field will automatically become your DUZ, the DATE ENTERED field
will automatically become 'TODAY's date. Each time the TEXT of a frame
is updated, the DATE LAST UPDATED field will be changed.
 
On entering the text, any words surrounded by brackets  [[  ]]  will 
automatically become keywords. These keywords must be tied into 
RELATED FRAMES. This is done by filling in the multiple field RELATED 
FRAME KEYWORD.
 
If a keyword appears in the text and not the related frame list, or
vice versa, this will be indicated on attempting to edit the related
frames.
