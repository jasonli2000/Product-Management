AMERICA, AMERICAN and JOURNAL cannot be used as significant
words or word fragments if they appear in the MIDDLE of a title;
however, they can be used for look-up if they appear as the first
word of a title, as a form of [TRUNCATION].
 
If you enter a word or a word fragment, you will get a numbered
list of choices from which to select the correct title.  For
example:
 
Select UNION LIST OF PERIODICALS TITLE: <PSY><CR>
     1 PSYCHIATRIC MEDICINE
     2 PSYCHOLOGY TODAY
     3 PSYCHIATRY  ARCHIVES OF GENERAL PSYCHIATRY
     4 PSYCHOLOGY  JOURNAL OF CLINICAL PSYCHOLOGY
Choose:1-4 <4><CR>
