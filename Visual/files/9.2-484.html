TEXT 
Occasionally, you will encounter some areas of confusion in selecting the
correct title from the Library ULP:
 
1.  The same title may appear in the database [TWICE], either because
    (1) it is both the old and new name of a journal (and a different
    name was used for that journal in the intervening years), or
    (2) two different publishers may have used the same journal name.
    There may also be a version of the title for the supplement as a
    separate title entry.
 
2.  Two other areas that might cause some confusion are the use of
[PUNCTUATION] marks and use of the [AMPERSAND].
