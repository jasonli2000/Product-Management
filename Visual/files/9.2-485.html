The majority if medical titles in the database are taken from SERLINE
and follow SERLINE conventions.  SERLINE's use of punctuation is
difficult to predict.  For example, SERLINE never uses the
apostrophe in titles.  Thus, TODAY'S NURSING HOME is entered as:
TODAYS NURSING HOME.
 
However, SERLINE does use the following punctuation:
       .  ,  ;  /  -  :
 
General titles not in SERLINE are taken from Ulrich's, which does
use the apostrophe.
 
If the SYNONYM field was not already used, the title with the
variant form of punctuation (or lack of it) was entered there.
