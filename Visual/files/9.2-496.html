History of Check-in (HIS) displays detailed information related to
receipt and disposition of specific issues.  It includes Category, 
Status, Location, Date Received, and Library Site Location information 
for each copy.
  
At the Select LOCAL SERIALS prompt, retrieve the title by entering
one of the following:
     (1)  significant word or word fragment
     (2)  ISSN/ISBN number
     (3)  NLM title abbreviation
If a numbered list of choices appear, select the correct title by
number.
