NAME XUUSER-DEACT
HEADER USING THE 'Deactivate user' OPTION
TEXT 
The 'Deactivate user' option terminates a user's access to the system.
You may request termination for a future date, or terminate immediately.
 
You will first be prompted for the termination date.  If the date you enter
is today, the access code will be deleted immediately.  Otherwise,
the termination date is recorded, and a nightly-run utility will
remove the access codes for users on their termination date.
 
On termination of a user, you may chose to purge all their mail, remove
them from any mail groups, and recall their keys.  It is recommended
that you respond to the 'DELETE...' prompts with a 'YES', unless it
is likely that a user will be re-activated.
