TEXT 
For faster retrieval, begin entries with unique letters or numbers.
For example, avoid beginning with "Ward."  This field accepts from
3-30 alpha-numeric characters for each entry; it will not accept 
a completely numeric response.
  
After the Service, section, or ward name has been entered and confirmed,
the system will prompt for the Hospital Service Mail Delivery Point Symbol
(MDP) routing number or designation, such as 142D, 136, etc.
