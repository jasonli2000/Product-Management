Name Value
NAME LBRY SUB
HEADER SUBJECT SETUP
TEXT 
Subject headings, which will be assigned to individual titles
in Holdings Setup (HOL), are a local decision.  Each library
should develop its own list in advance and then enter the assigned
subject headings here.
  
Subject headings must be entered in this option before they can be
used in HOL.
  
At the Select SUBJECT NAME prompt, enter the term.
  
Subjects may be edited but not deleted.
