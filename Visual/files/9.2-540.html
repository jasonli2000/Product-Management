1.  START DATES and STOP DATES must be input in the form of M/D/Y.
  
2.  START DATES and STOP DATES relate to the JOURNAL DATE
(issue date) field in Check-In (CHE).  Thus, if the Check-In
option is to accept a new copy of a title which begins to arrive
with the April 1988 issue, the START DATE must be 4/1/88 or
earlier.
  
3.  The STOP DATE must be a least one day after the copy's START DATE.
