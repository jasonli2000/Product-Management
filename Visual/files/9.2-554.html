TEXT 
The Serials Titles Reports (STR) option provides for the creation of
numerous reports of interest to library staff; some of them may be
printed out and distributed to library patrons.  The reports are related 
to missing issues; the location, format, source, or category of the titles;
new titles which were not previously in the Title Authority File; and
subscription and cost information by category.  Information for these
reports is taken from the data entered in the Library Serials Management
(LSM) and Serials Control (SER) portions of the Library Management module.
