TEXT 
Use the Missing Issues List (MIL) option to display the issues
identified as missing from the Check-in (CHE) option.
  
The list will sort alphabetically by the vendor's name.  All the
individual missing issues for subscriptions placed with that vendor will
be arranged alphabetically by title with the oldest missing issue printed
first under the title.  The number of copies missing, volume and issue,
ISSN, and date of the issue will also be displayed.
  
Please enter [START] for information on tailoring this report to
display a range of entries.
