NAME LBRY TITLES ALPHABETICAL
HEADER ALL SERIALS TITLES
TEXT 
All Serials Titles (AST) displays the serials holdings for the local
library.  It may be used to generate a list to be distributed to patrons,
as well as for library staff use.
  
The list will be arranged alphabetically by title of the serial
and include Former or Continued By titles for those serials which have
changed their names, ISSN, location, category, general notes, and 
microfilm and hardcopy holdings information.
  
Enter [HOLDINGS] to see how to get brief or detailed holdings
printed out and enter [DEVICE] to see how to print the list.
