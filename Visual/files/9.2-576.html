You can produce three differently formatted reports using the List of
Recipients (REC) option.
 
1.  Accepting the YES default at the "Do you want a list of recipients
arranged by titles ?" prompt will display an alphabetic list of titles
with all the recipients of the title.
 
2.  NO entered at the prompt will generate a list of the individual
recipients with all the titles routed to them listed alphabetically
under each name.
