Additional features to be included in subsequent versions of the Serials
Module include:
  
(1) bar-coding to facilitate circulation and the collection of usage
    figures;
(2) a binding option to track the binding process;
(3) a microformat option to track the ordering and receipt of reels of
    microfilm;
(4) linkages to IFCAP; and
(5) usage statistics.
  
The SIUG is very interested in your comments, suggestions and criticisms.
Please be sure to convey these, along with your ideas and questions, 
to the SIUG members.
