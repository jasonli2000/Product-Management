In most of the options of the Serials Module, you need to find 
or "pull up" a title as the first action.  There are a number of
ways that this can be done; some are better than others.
  
It is recommended that you use one of these three methods:
   (1)  entering a significant word or word fragment;
   (2)  entering the ISSN or ISBN; or
   (3)  entering the NLM title abbreviation.
  
AMERICA, AMERICAN, and JOURNAL cannot be used as significant words or
word fragments if they appear in the middle of a title; however, they
can be used for pull-up if they appear as the first word of a title.
