You use the Title Authority File (TAF) to develop your Local Serials File
by selecting titles from the TAF.
  
If you find and pull up a title already in the TAF (a national entry), 
you must accept the bibliographic data which comes with it whether or 
not it is complete or accurate.  You may only view this data; you may 
not edit it.
  
Please [CONTINUE] to see how to handle a new title that is not in 
the TAF.
