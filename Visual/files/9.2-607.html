If the title is not in the national Title Authority
File (TAF), then as you create it, you are adding it to a local
portion of the TAF.  While it is in this local portion, the TITLE
field may be edited, but not deleted.  The other fields of bibliographic
data to accompany this new title may be both edited and deleted.
  
Periodically you will be requested to submit a listing of these titles
in the local portion of the TAF to a central point for verification
and addition to the national portion of the TAF.  When the next
up-dated TAF tape is received, these titles will be included
and you will no longer be able to edit them locally.
