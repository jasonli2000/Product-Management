Assistance in the use of this Module is available in several forms:
(1) Help Prompts; (2) Help [FRAMES]; and (3) advice and trouble-shooting 
by [SIUG] members.
  
Help Prompts are short statements of explanation available at every
prompt if a ? is entered at that point.  For example, at the field
INACTIVE, type a ? to pull up a short explanation of what this field
means or requires.
