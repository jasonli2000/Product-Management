TEXT 
The fastest way to completely exit the computer system and close
down your terminal is to back up to the nearest "Select XXXX Option"
prompt and enter HA for the word HALT.  The system will then ask you
to confirm that you want to halt.  For example,
  
Select Serials Control Option:  <HA> <CR>
Do you really want to halt? YES// <CR>
