HEADER Prediction Pop-Up
TEXT 
The prediction pop-up default is set for 5 days.  If a journal dated August
1988 is expected to be received during the last five days of July, do not
enter anything here.  If a journal dated August 1988 is expected to arrive
ten days before that date, enter 10.  For example, if POPULAR SCIENCE which
is dated with a month and year only (July 1988) consistently arrives
around the middle of the previous month, enter 15 here.
