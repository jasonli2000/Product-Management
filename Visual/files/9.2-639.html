If you determined a title does not exist in the TITLE AUTHORITY
FILE (TAF), you must add it locally.  The new title will be added to
both the TITLE AUTHORITY and LOCAL SERIALS files when the following
action is taken:
  
At the Select TITLE AUTHORITY prompt, enter the full name of the 
serial title in all capital letters, omitting initial articles.
Use SERLINE, UCMP, ULRICH'S, and the journal itself, in that order, 
for bibliographic verification.
