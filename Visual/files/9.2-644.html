PLACEMENT OF ITEM:  This refers to the standard locations for placement
         of journals once they are checked in.  These standard locations 
         must first be entered in the Item Placement Location (IPL) option
         before they can be entered in Copy Specific Edit (CSE).  These
         locations should include the names of Services for administrative
         titles.
  
CATEGORY OF COPY:  Select MEDICAL, GENERAL, RESEARCH, ADMINISTRATIVE, or
         PATIENT HEALTH EDUCATION.  The "Category" designation will be used
         to sort information in some of the "reports" options under Serials
         Control (SER).
