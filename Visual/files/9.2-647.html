TEXT 
Select CATEGORY:  Optional; multiple.  Choices are limited to 
     M for Medical, G for General, R for Research, A for Administrative,
     or P for Patient Health Education.  This is a multiple field,
     enabling a category for each copy.  A library may have, for example,
     two copies of PSYCHOLOGY TODAY, 1 Medical and 1 Patient Health
     Education.
  
Reports may be printed out sorted by category.
