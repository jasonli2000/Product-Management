To retrieve the title of an issue which is ready for check-in,
enter one of the following at the Select LOCAL SERIALS prompt:
     (1)  significant word or word fragment from the title;
     (2)  ISSN/ISBN number; or
     (3)  NLM title abbreviation.
  
If a numbered list of choices appears, select the correct title
by number.  For example:
  
Select LOCAL SERIALS:  <PSY><CR>
     1  PSYCHIATRIC MEDICINE
     2  PSYCHOLOGY TODAY
     3  PSYCHIATRY  ARCHIVES OF GENERAL PSYCHIATRY
     4  PSYCHOLOGY  JOURNAL OF CLINICAL PSYCHOLOGY
  
Choose 1-4:<2><CR>
