NAME LBRY CHECK-IN EDIT ??
HEADER SERIALS CHECK-IN CHOICES
TEXT 
Enter a number under heading "ID NUM" to check-in a journal date.
 
"I" allows you to (I)nsert a new journal date.
 
"E" or E("ID NUM") allows you to (E)dit a journal date.
 
"N" allows you to see (N)otes about this journal.
 
"B" will display the previous five journal dates.  You may enter
B("ID NUM") to start at that journal date and see five journal dates.
 
"F" will display the next five journal dates.  You may enter F("ID NUM")
to start at that journal date and see five journal dsates.
