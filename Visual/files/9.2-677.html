NAME LBRY CSE CHOICE PROMPT
HEADER COPY SPECIFIC EDIT CHOICES
TEXT 
"I" allows you to (I)nsert a new copy or a ToC.
 
"E" allows you to (E)dit a copy or a ToC.  You may enter
E("ID NUM") to select that copy or ToC to edit.  Here you
can set a START DATE and/or a STOP DATE for any copy or
ToC.
 
"R" allows you to (R)emove any copy or ToC not being used
in any journal date.  You may enter R("ID NUM") to select
a copy or ToC to remove that entry.
 
"B" will display the previous five copies.  You may enter
B("ID NUM") to start at that copy and see five copies.
 
"F" will display the next five copies.  You may enter
F("ID NUM") to start at that copy and see five copies.
