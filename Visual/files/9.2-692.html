HEADER Event Capture Active Procedures
TEXT 
This prompt from the active procedures option allows you to select the
order of presentation for the National procedures.  You may select to
present the National procedures in either alphabetical order by procedure
name, or in numberical order by National Number.
 
If you have selected a report of both National and Local procedures, the
Local procedures will be presented in alphabetical order by procedure name
regardless of the option you choose for the presentation of the National
Procedures.
