The Event Capture Software offers a variety of options for entering and
reporting data through Event Capture for the Decision Support System.  
You must have the appropriate security key to see the management and
spreadsheet menus; otherwise, you will only see the data entry menu
and selective reports.
 
The interactive help portion of this option lets you quickly navigate 
to specific help frames within the Event Capture software by first 
selecting a menu, then selecting the applicable option from that menu.
