NAME XUDOC SPOOL USE1
HEADER USING THE SPOOLER
TEXT 
Have you ever used the spooler?  If not, you might like to know the reasons
for spooling.  What are the advantages?  [WHY SPOOL?]
 
To create a spool document, just send it to the spool device by responding
to the device prompt as follows:
 
     DEVICE: SPOOL
 
If your system has several networked processors, you may need to specify
where you would like spooling to occur.  If you are not queuing, choose the
one on your current CPU.
 
     DEVICE: SPOOL
 
      1   SPOOL AAA
      2   SPOOL BBB
     Choose 1-2>
 
To queue, respond to the device prompt with 'Q'.  This will allow use of a
spooler on another CPU.  It will also allow spooling to occur at a later
time.                                                            [CONTINUE]
