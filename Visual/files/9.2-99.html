NAME XUDOC SPOOL USE4
HEADER PRINTING SPOOLED DOCUMENTS
TEXT 
How do you print out what you've spooled?  Where did the job go when you
sent it to the spooler?  How can you get it back?
 
     Spooling --------- sends to --------------> the spooler (spool space).
                                                 =================
                                                 |"your document"|
                                                 =================
                                                  /
                Printing ---- gets your document /
                              from the spooler and
                                               /
                                              /
                                         sends it to
                                            /
                                           /
                                a printer (or several printers).
 
Try printing.  Use a margin of 132 characters and page length of 66 lines
to match the spool format even though your document may not be this big.
Then specify the number of copies, and the printing device.  If you need to
use several printers, the device prompt will reappear and you may respond.
