NAME VA-ALCOHOL ABUSE COMPLICATIONS
PRINT NAME Alcohol Abuse Complications
EDUCATIONAL OUTCOME 
The patient and family will explain how to avoid the complications of
alcohol dependency and slow the progression of the diesease by compliance
with a prescribed daily program.
EDUCATIONAL STANDARDS 
1.  Review the effects that the alcohol has on the body. 
Emphasize the long term effects of the patients continued use of alcohol.
 
2.  Discuss the symptoms indicative of progression of disease.
 
3.  Review the effects of alcohol dependency on all family members.
