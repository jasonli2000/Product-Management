NAME VA-DIABETES MEDICATIONS
PRINT NAME Diabetes Medications
EDUCATIONAL OUTCOME 
The patient will recognize the importance medication compliance plays in
controlling diabetes and its complications.
EDUCATIONAL STANDARDS 
1.  Review the two types of diabetes medications.  Explain their indication,
mechanism of action, and possible side effects.
 
2.  Review the symptoms of hypoglycemia and how to treat a hypoglycemic
reaction.
 
3.  If using insulin, demonstrate the proper way to prepare and administer an
insulin injection and how to perform a finger stick to check glucose level
using a glucometer.  Have patient return demonstration.
