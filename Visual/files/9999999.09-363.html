NAME VA-EXERCISE
PRINT NAME Exercise
EDUCATIONAL OUTCOME 
The patient will relate exercise and or physical fitness to health promotion
and disease prevention.
EDUCATIONAL STANDARDS 
1.  Review the benefits of regular exercise.
 
2.  Discuss the three types of exercise: aerobic, flexibility, and endurance.
 
3.  Review the basic recommendations for any exercise program:
    a.  If any chronic health problems exist, consult with provider.
    b.  Start out slow.
    c.  Exercise a minimum of three times a week.
 
4.  Review the exercise programs available in the community/VA resources.
