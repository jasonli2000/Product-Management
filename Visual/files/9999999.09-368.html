NAME VA-SELF BREAST EXAM
PRINT NAME Self Breast Exam
EDUCATIONAL OUTCOME 
The patient will recognize the importance of monthly breast
self-examination.
EDUCATIONAL STANDARDS 
1.  Discuss breast anatomy and the normal changes that occurs with
menstruation and aging.
 
2.  Emphasize the importance of monthly examination in early detection of
breast cancer.  Survival rates are markedly higher when cancer is detected and
treated early.
 
3.  Teach breast self-exam.  Have the patient give a return demonstration.
 
4.  Discuss indications for mammography.  Patients who have first degree
relatives (mother, sister, or daughter) with breast cancer are encouraged to
have baseline and follow-up mammograms.
