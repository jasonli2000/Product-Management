VA-SUNSCREEN (370)    EDUCATION TOPICS (9999999.09)

Name Value
NAME VA-SUNSCREEN
PRINT NAME Sunscreen
EDUCATIONAL OUTCOME 
The patient will recognize the effects of prolonged sun exposure on the
skin.
EDUCATIONAL STANDARDS 
1.  Explain how prolonged sun exposure, especially between the hours of 10AM
and 2PM, damages the skin.
 
2.  Discuss the appearance of sun damaged skin.
 
3.  Explain the importance of consulting a provider for skin lesions that are
new, changing in appearance, painful, or not healing.
 
4.  Discuss the use of sunscreen before going outside and repeated applications
for prolonged exposure.
 
5.  Discuss the use of long sleeved shirts and using wide brimmed hats for
scalp and face protection.
