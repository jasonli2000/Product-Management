NAME VA-SUBSTANCE ABUSE FOLLOW-UP
PRINT NAME Substance Abuse Follow-up
EDUCATIONAL OUTCOME 
The patient and family will explain how participation in a support program
may prevent relapse.
EDUCATIONAL STANDARDS 
1.  Discuss the philosophy of AA, ALANON, and related groups.
 
2.  Review the nature of chemical dependency as a primary, chronic, and
arrestable disease.
 
3.  Review the treatment and support options available to the patient and
family.
