EDUCATIONAL OUTCOME 
This topic represents the inquiry process done by a clinician to
determine if the patient should receive Advance Directives education.
 
This topic was created as an example of a screening topic that could be
used on an Encounter Form, for check-off by the clinician.
EDUCATIONAL STANDARDS 
This standard is actually a guideline.  Clinicians could use this
guideline as an example of the type of questions to ask the patient when
the clinician needs to determine whether the patient should receive
Advance Directives education.
 
Ask the patient if he or she has ever discussed his or her wishes
concerning aggressive medical care during the end of life or a persistent
vegetative state, and if these discussions have even been made formal in
any signed document.
